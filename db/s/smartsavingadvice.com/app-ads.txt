<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Does_Robinhood_Accept_PayPal_or_Venmo"></span>Does Robinhood Accept PayPal or Venmo?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>

Venmo is not accepted or supported by Robinhood.&nbsp;</strong></p><!-- Ezoic - wp_mid_content - mid_content --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-112" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="mid_content"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(112); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_mid_content - mid_content -->

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Does_Robinhood_Accept_Credit_or_Debit_Cards"></span>Does Robinhood Accept Credit or Debit Cards?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p><strong>Robinhood does not accept credit cards or debit cards for deposits but you can make a deposit with the account your debit card is linked to.&nbsp;</strong></p>



<p>According to Robinhood, you can conveniently verify if you have a valid checking account and then deposit from that account.&nbsp;</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_longest_content - longest_content --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-115" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="longest_content"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(115); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_longest_content - longest_content -->



<p>Robinhood prefers checking over savings and money market accounts. You may encounter issues when linking a savings account and transferring money to Robinhood.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Similarly, you may also experience problems connecting savings and checking accounts of relatively smaller banks. These probabilities are according to Robinhood&#x2019;s self-published disclosures on their <a href="https://robinhood.com/us/en/support/articles/link-your-bank-account/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">official support pages</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Does_Robinhood_Accept_Cash_App"></span>Does Robinhood Accept Cash App?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>

You will use what is essentially a backdoor entry to connect the two interfaces.&nbsp;</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_incontent_5 - incontent_5 --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-116" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="incontent_5"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(116); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_incontent_5 - incontent_5 -->



<p><strong>Robinhood has not issued any press release or officially announced in any support articles if it accepts Cash App. However, you may be able to link your Cash App account and routing numbers with Robinhood.&nbsp;</strong></p>



<p>Some users on <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/CashApp/comments/ibbfrv/cashapp_and_robinhood_transfers/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Reddit</a> and elsewhere have figured out one probable reason why Cash App works on Robinhood. While neither service is a traditional saving or checking account, both applications use Sutton Bank.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Thus, the two services have interoperability because of the bank. This is similar to why PayPal&#x2019;s Cash Card worked with Robinhood, courtesy of Wells Fargo.&nbsp;</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_incontent_6 - incontent_6 --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-117" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="incontent_6"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(117); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_incontent_6 - incontent_6 -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Get_Your_Cash_App_Account_Routing_Numbers"></span>Get Your Cash App Account &amp; Routing Numbers<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Take these steps to get your Cash App account and routing numbers:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list"><li>Log in to your Cash App.</li><li>Go to My Cash.</li><li>Choose Cash.</li><li>Get Account Number.</li><li>Enable Account.</li></ol>



<p>Once you select Enable Account, Cash App will display the routing and account numbers on the subsequent display. You may note these details or copy the information to paste later.&nbsp;</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_incontent_7 - incontent_7 --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-118" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="incontent_7"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(118); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_incontent_7 - incontent_7 -->



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Add_Cash_App_to_Linked_Accounts_on_Robinhood"></span>Add Cash App to Linked Accounts on Robinhood<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Take these steps to add Cash App to Robinhood linked accounts:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list"><li>Sign in to your Robinhood app.</li><li>Go to Account.&nbsp;</li><li>Choose Transfers.</li><li>Select Linked Accounts.</li><li>Add a New Account.</li><li>Go to More Banks.</li><li>Check &#x201C;I don&#x2019;t see my bank.&#x201D;</li><li>Select Checking or Savings.</li><li>Add the Cash App account &amp; routing numbers.</li></ol>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Link_Robinhood_Debit_Card_With_Cash_App_Account"></span>Link Robinhood Debit Card With Cash App Account<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>You need a Robinhood debit card for this step. Signing up for Robinhood&#x2019;s <a href="https://robinhood.com/us/en/support/articles/get-started-with-cash-management/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cash Management</a> service provides you with their debit MasterCard automatically issued by Sutton Bank.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Cash App <a href="https://cash.app/help/hk/en-us/5588-supported-cards" target="_blank" rel="noopener">supports</a> MasterCard and other debit or credit cards issued by major brands. <h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Two-Way_Funds_Transfer_Between_Cash_App_and_Robinhood"></span>Two-Way Funds Transfer Between Cash App and Robinhood<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
