<p>Welcome to StatsCrew.com, the home of sports statistics from a wide variety of North American professional leagues. We currently host stats for professional baseball, football, hockey, basketball, soccer and lacrosse. We are also adding statistics for minor leagues and will continue to add new leagues and sports to the site.</p>

<p>The Stats Crew is very proud to have brought online the most complete <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/football/l-CFL">Canadian Football League statistics database</a> available anywhere. In addition to the Canadian game, we've cast our attention Down Under as the <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/aussiefootball/l-AUSFL">Australian Football League</a> has joined our lineup.</p>

<p>StatsCrew.com also features the Internet's largest selection of <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/soccer">US outdoor and indoor soccer stats</a>, all fully searchable and sortable by player and team. We have further added to our soccer statistics by assembling a one-of-a-kind <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/womenssoccer">professional women's soccer database</a>.</p>

<p>In order to cover players' complete pro careers, the Stats Crew has incorporated data from top alternative professional leagues such as the <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/football/l-USFL">United States Football League</a>, <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/basketball/l-ABA">American Basketball Association</a> and <a href="https://www.statscrew.com/hockey/l-WHA">World Hockey Association</a> into its datasets.</p>

<p>If you need stats, if no one else can help, and if you can find them, maybe you can ask the Stats Crew.</p>
