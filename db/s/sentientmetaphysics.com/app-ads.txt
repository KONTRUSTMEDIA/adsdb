<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Baked apple poultice – a sore throat, inflammations, fever, replenished intestinal flora – After a course of antibiotics leave a dish of apple sauce and let stand overnight and consume the next day.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Apple peel – heartburn. Crushed apple pulp – inflammations or small flesh wounds. Grated apple – aids digestion and reduces acidity in the stomach. Apple seed – chew for lung complaints.</p>
