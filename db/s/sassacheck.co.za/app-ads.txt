Your grant will be deposited directly into your account on your assigned payment date.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Option_2_CashSend_Services"></span>Option 2: CashSend Services<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>No bank account? You can collect your cash at Pick n Pay or Boxer Stores using CashSend. Each result has a specific meaning and tells you what action to take next.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Pending_Status"></span>Pending Status<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>The most common outcome. Your SRD grant status is under verification. This is a standard procedure and can take up to 90 days to complete. No action is needed from your side during this phase.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Approved_Status"></span>Approved Status<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>Your SASSA SRD grant status is confirmed. You will receive an SMS with your SASSA payment dates and collection point. SASSA now conducts automatic monthly eligibility reviews, so you do not need to reapply — just keep your details updated.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Status_Declined_Identity_Verification_Failed"></span>Status Declined: Identity Verification Failed<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>This means your SASSA Status Check failed during the identity verification process. Your documents did not pass DHA (Department of Home Affairs) verification. Common causes include:</p><ul><li>Incorrect ID number entered</li><li>Mismatched personal details</li><li>Outdated identity documents</li></ul><p>You can appeal this decision through the reconsideration platform.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Alternative_Income_Source_Identified"></span>Alternative Income Source Identified<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>SASSA detected that you are earning income from other sources. To qualify as a SASSA grant beneficiary, you must be unemployed or classified as vulnerable. If this information is incorrect, lodge an appeal with proof of your employment status.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Means_Income_Source_Identified_Undefined"></span>Means Income Source Identified Undefined<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>This outcome is less serious. Resubmit your application with complete information about any income sources.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Self-Exclusion_Response_Found"></span>Self-Exclusion Response Found<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>You marked yourself as a &#8220;Voluntary Opt-out&#8221; during the application. If this was done in error, contact SASSA to reverse the decision.</p><h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Payday_Null"></span>Payday Null<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3><p>Your SRD Status Check shows approved, but no payment date has been assigned.
