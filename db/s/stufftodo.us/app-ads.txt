		<h1 class="entry-title">Stuff to do in the United States</h1>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		
<p>Welcome to the United States! The land of the free and the home of the brave, where you can find just about everything your heart desires. Whether you&#8217;re a thrill-seeking adventurer or a culture vulture looking to immerse yourself in the local scene, there&#8217;s cool and fun stuff to do for everyone in this diverse and vibrant country.  We want to help you find cool stuff to do and tell you where to travel in the USA!</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image size-large is-resized"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" src="https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-1024x616.jpg" alt="where to travel in the united states" class="wp-image-457" width="551" height="331" srcset="https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-1024x616.jpg 1024w, https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-300x180.jpg 300w, https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-768x462.jpg 768w, https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-1536x924.jpg 1536w, https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-2048x1233.jpg 2048w, https://stufftodo.us/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/statue-of-liberty-850x512.jpg 850w" sizes="(max-width: 551px) 100vw, 551px" /></figure>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Where to travel in the United States</strong></h3>



<p>If you&#8217;re looking for a good time, head straight to <a href="https://stufftodo.us/las-vegas/">Las Vegas</a>. The neon lights never go out and the party never stops. If you want to try your luck at the blackjack table or catch a show by one of the world&#8217;s biggest stars, Vegas has it all. For those feeling especially adventurous, you can take a helicopter tour of the Grand Canyon.</p>



<p>If you prefer a more laid-back vacation, head to the beaches of <a href="https://stufftodo.us/florida/">Florida</a>.  Here you can soak up the sun, ride the waves, and enjoy some of the best seafood in the country. If you are feeling adventurous, you can visit Florida&#8217;s many theme parks, including Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld.</p>



<p>For something a bit more cultural, visit New York City, the city that never sleeps. Here you can take in a Broadway show, visit world-famous museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art and the Guggenheim, and indulge in some of the best shopping and dining in the world.</p>



<p>If you&#8217;re a history buff, you can&#8217;t miss a trip to Washington D.C. Here you can visit iconic landmarks like the White House, the Lincoln Memorial, and the Washington Monument. For a taste of the Wild West, visit the Grand Canyon in Arizona. Here you can take in breathtaking views of the natural wonder and learn about the rich history of the area.</p>



<p>If you&#8217;re looking for a bit of everything, visit California. Here you can experience everything from the glitz and glamour of Hollywood to the natural beauty of the Pacific Coast Highway.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>The United States has it all and tons of stuff to do in every state</strong></h3>



<p>No matter where you go in the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">United States</a>, you&#8217;re sure to have a blast! Remember to pack your sense of adventure and your sense of humor when you visit the USA.  You never know what kind of crazy situations you might find yourself in and you will be glad you came. Happy travels!</p>
