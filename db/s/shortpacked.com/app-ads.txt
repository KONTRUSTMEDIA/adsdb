<p><a href="/uploads/1691174980-Strongarm4.jpg"><img src="/uploads/1691174980-Strongarm4.jpg" style="width: 50%; float: left;" class="note-float-left"></a>

Last time, we <b><a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/our-worlds-are-in-danger" target="_blank">talked about Override</a></b>, and today we're talking about <i>Legacy</i> Deluxe Class Elita One (spelled "Elita-1" on the packaging because Hasbro doesn't care about parsing Transformers names correctly the way I do), so we're going from Lady Transformer With Head Hat On Car Roof to Lady Transformer to Head Hat On Car Roof!&nbsp; Those lady Transformers sure like putting their head hats on car roofs!&nbsp; Elita's is removeable (and isn't her actual forehead, unlike Override's) and can plug into anything with a 5mm port.&nbsp; It's just there to make her vehicle mode look more like her animation model.</p><p>Though technically it makes her vehicle mode look more like... fan art of her animation model?&nbsp; For decades, we didn't have access to Elita One's actual animation model (and we only see the front of it in her one episode), so <b><a href="https://hero.fandom.com/wiki/Elita-One?file=Elita-1_G1_cartoon.png" target="_blank">at least one enterprising fan artist</a></b> took a crack at it, and arrived at a very short car mode that... this toy seems to be based on?&nbsp; It was only in the past year or so that we've been blessed with <b><a href="https://tfwiki.net/wiki/File:Elita_One_in_vehicle_mode.jpg" target="_blank">an actual animation model</a></b> and the... much more elongated actual car mode Elita One was designed to have.&nbsp; &nbsp;I mean, both the fan art and the actual model have the head hat on the roof, but, like, dang.&nbsp; Hasbro.&nbsp; Check your sources.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p><a href="/uploads/1662435925-Legacy Elita1.jpg"><img src="/uploads/1662435925-Legacy Elita1.jpg" style="width: 50%; float: right;" class="note-float-right"></a>

This is the first Elita One toy to be based on her animation model while also transforming into a car!&nbsp; We've got a <b><a href="https://tfwiki.net/wiki/File:POTP-Voyager-Elita-1.jpg" target="_blank">retool of Starscream from back in<i> Power of the Primes</i></a></b>, and that became a jet, and I still adore that toy, but this Elita One is getting back to basics.&nbsp; She's a car!&nbsp; She's a car with her forehead on the roof.&nbsp; The forehead on the roof is important.&nbsp; Did her <i>Power of the Primes</i> jet mode have her forehead on the fuselage?&nbsp; No!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>After that weird half-step of <b><a href="https://tfwiki.net/wiki/File:WFC_Trilogy_Elita-1.jpeg" target="_blank">Netflix's Elita One</a></b>, it's also nice to have an Elita One who, like, looks like Elita One.&nbsp; Her torso's actually got sculpted detail that looks like her!&nbsp; She's got her collar and the T on her tummy, and the crotch fangs... it's all well-appreciated.&nbsp; She does borrow Netflix Elita One's shoulders, but that's fine.&nbsp; And also her legs ... become the actual back of the vehicle, rather than being slim lady legs that tuck underneath somewhere?&nbsp; That's more than fine!&nbsp; It's off-model, but it's way preferable.&nbsp; I love some good stomping boots.&nbsp; One Arcee is more than enough, we don't need a second lady Transformer that piles the entire vehicle mode on their ass just so that they can have shapely calves.</p>

<p><a href="/uploads/1662435937-Elita and Female Autobots.jpg"><img src="/uploads/1662435937-Elita and Female Autobots.jpg" style="width: 50%; float: left;" class="note-float-left"></a>

Elita One comes with two rifles: one that's based on her pistol from the cartoon, but elongated into a more substantial weapon, and one that's <b><a href="https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Minerva_(G1_robot)" target="_blank">Minerva</a></b>'s.&nbsp; Elita One is going to be retooled later as Minerva, with a new headsculpt and presumably released as a Walgreens exclusive.&nbsp; (they like their medic Transformer exclusives)&nbsp; So that Minerva weapon's built in.&nbsp; Elita One's backpack also has two configurations for this reason.&nbsp; You can bunch it all that car hood stuff up behind her, out of sight, or you can extend the outer thirds into taller shoulders that kinda-sorta-mostly remind one of Minerva's taller car door shoulders.&nbsp; A similar silhouette, if you will.&nbsp; I'll use that configuration on Minerva, while keeping Elita One's backpack compact.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>My only real complaint about this toy is... it's not tall enough?&nbsp; Elita One is, like, <b><a href="File:SearchAlphaTrion Prime Elita Autobots.JPG" target="_blank">Ironhide height</a></b>, while this toy is regular Autobot car height.&nbsp; A weird misstep after years of hitting scale at any cost.&nbsp; And it's not like she's smaller to synchronize with Minerva -- Minerva's Ironhide height, too!&nbsp; She <b><a href="https://tfwiki.net/wiki/File:Minerva_victory.jpg" target="_blank">stood next to Perceptor once</a></b>.&nbsp; Anyway.&nbsp; I dunno.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><br>A very satisfying Transformer, regardless.&nbsp; She's a female Transformer but, like, with vehicle mode parts that become her limbs.&nbsp; whaaaaaaaaaat?????</p></div><div class="cc-tagline">Tags: <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/search/generations">generations</a>, <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/search/legacy">legacy</a>, <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/search/elita one">elita one</a></div><div class="cc-commentlink"><a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/car-hat#disqus_thread" data-disqus-identifier="blog-1032">View/Post Comments</a></div><script id="dsq-count-scr" src="//shortpacked.disqus.com/count.js" async></script></article><div class="cc-prevnext"><a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/page/2">Next&nbsp;&gt;</a></div><div class="cc-pagelist">Page 1 <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/page/2">2</a> <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/page/3">3</a> <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/page/4">4</a> <a href="https://www.shortpacked.com/blog/page/104">... 104</a></div><div style="clear:both;
