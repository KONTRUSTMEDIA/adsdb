Through apps such as UU Paotui, users can hire workers to queue at restaurants, accompany them to hospital appointments, feed pets, or complete errands.</p><p>However, a commentary in the Communist Party’s People’s Daily newspaper described the bowing service as “very awkward” and called for closer scrutiny of the expanding proxy service industry.</p><p>“Real innovation should meet needs while
