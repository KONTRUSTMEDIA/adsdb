<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="1_Baylor_University"></span>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Baylor University<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Baylor University is a private non-profit 4-year school located in Waco Texas. On this campus, academic excellence is elevated, a Christian commitment is celebrated, research is cultivated and the Baylor Family welcomes each student with open arms. Their application fee is quick, simple, and free for either their incoming students or transfer students. Baylor&#8217;s academic environment offers a place where you can learn and grow. Here, smaller classes mean that faculty know your name, office hours provide open doors to build relationships and a challenging curriculum leads to preparation for success.</p>

<p>Established in 1889, Texas A&amp;M University-Commerce proudly maintains a 133-year legacy of excellence. A&amp;M-Commerce is the third-largest university in the prestigious Texas A&amp;M University System. At Texas A&amp;M University-Commerce, we transform lives through our mission to “Educate. Discover. Achieve.” With locations all over Dallas and East Texas, they deliver 130 unique academic degrees and serve almost 11,000 students. </p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="3_San_Jacinto_Community_College"></span>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; San Jacinto Community College<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Established in 1961, San Jacinto College has created a deep impact on the East Harris County region. What started as two rented buildings in downtown Pasadena, has grown to five campuses and more than 90,000 graduates. Along the way, their journey has been filled with many firsts and accomplishments. 20,826 are attending San Jac to earn an associate degree, 5,752 are attending to earn credits for transferring to a four-year institution and 2,053 are working toward a certificate.</p>



<p> At San Jac, will you find over 200 degrees, certificates, and continuing education programs to choose from. You’ll get the chance to study underneath nationally recognized faculty. You’ll receive hands-on learning in professional-style settings. And you’ll get the keys to where you want to go next. The application is free for the school and there is no deadline.</p>
