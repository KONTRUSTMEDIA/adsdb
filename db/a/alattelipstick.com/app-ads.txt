                <p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-4592 size-full" src="/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/golds-gym-workout.png" alt="golds gym workout" width="480" height="640" srcset="/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/golds-gym-workout.png 480w, /wp-content/uploads/2017/09/golds-gym-workout-225x300.png 225w, /wp-content/uploads/2017/09/golds-gym-workout-300x400.png 300w" sizes="(max-width: 480px) 100vw, 480px" data-jpibfi-post-excerpt="" data-jpibfi-post-url="/2017/09/health-check-in-and-high-cholesterol/" data-jpibfi-post-title="Reminder to Why I Live Healthy &#8211; Checkup and High Cholesterol" data-jpibfi-src="/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/golds-gym-workout.png"></p>
              </blockquote>
              <p>This week we had a health and wellness screening at work where they took our vitals, tested our cholesterol, recorded our height and weight, and more. I absolutely hate seeing blood but for a discount on health insurance, I&nbsp;obliged and&nbsp;handed my finger over while closing my eyes tight.</p>
              <p>Overall my cholesterol was all right, &nbsp;but I was quite upset when I heard the broken down results. My good cholesterol was very good but my bad cholesterol was quite high.</p>
              <p>We did this exact exam last year and my cholesterol was great and there was no need for improvement. I was very reassured and happy about this because high cholesterol runs in my family. A glowing review&nbsp;was unfortunately not the case this year.</p>
