There are so many online <a class="wpil_keyword_link" href="https://accessbsl.com/bsl-courses/" title="BSL &#8211; British Sign Language Courses in the UK" data-wpil-keyword-link="linked" data-wpil-monitor-id="260714">BSL courses</a> that many new learners find it difficult to know what is an accredited course and what is not.</p>



<p class="has-ast-global-color-5-color has-text-color">We provide up to date information on <a href="https://accessbsl.com/endorsed-bsl-courses-whats-the-difference/" data-type="URL" data-id="https://accessbsl.com/endorsed-bsl-courses-whats-the-difference/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Ofqual courses</a> if you are looking to do a qualification (Level 1 &#8211; Level 6 BSL). You will find details of recommended online and classroom courses taught by qualified tutors. </p>



<p class="has-ast-global-color-5-color has-text-color">You will also find information about how to learn <a href="https://accessbsl.com/bsl-sse-or-makaton-whats-the-difference/" data-type="URL" data-id="https://accessbsl.com/bsl-sse-or-makaton-whats-the-difference/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">BSL</a> effectively, <a href="https://accessbsl.com/bsl-fingerspelling-tips/" data-type="URL" data-id="https://accessbsl.com/bsl-fingerspelling-tips/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">fingerspelling tips</a>, games to help with learning and resources for higher-level <a href="https://accessbsl.com/fingerspelling-alphabet-charts/" target="_blank" data-type="URL" data-id="https://accessbsl.com/fingerspelling-alphabet-charts/" rel="noreferrer noopener">BSL</a> qualifications. Our aim is to be a trusted resource for finding bona fide courses that will help you learn British Sign Language.</p>
