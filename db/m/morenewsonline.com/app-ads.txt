</span></div><div class="single-meta-bottom p-meta-info"></div></div></div></header><div class="single-top-section single-widget-section"></div><div class="single-body entry"><div class="single-content"><div class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody"><p>In an effort to empower Indian students aspiring to pursue higher education in the United States, the US Embassy launched the EducationUSA India mobile app on June 12th, 2019. This innovative app serves as a comprehensive guide, offering authentic information about courses, universities, and the application process in the USA.</p><h2><strong>EducationUSA App: Navigating the Path to US Universities</strong></h2><p>The primary goal of the EducationUSA app is to provide reliable information to students worldwide. Tailored specifically for Indian students, the app covers essential aspects such as the US education system, real-life stories from students studying in the USA, insights into student life, and additional resources for parents. Moreover, it aims to facilitate the application process by offering guidance and tracking capabilities.</p><p>The app serves as a centralized repository of authentic information on all accredited universities and the courses they offer in the USA. In 2018, a staggering 196,000 Indian students were enrolled in US universities, constituting 17% of the international student body.
