                    <br><h3 class="simple">
                        my grandpa appi gave me my first camcorder when i was about 8. my friends and i made movies on it every day after school in the back yard.
                        i still wanted to make movies when my friends were gone, so i got into lego stop-motion!
                        i eventually figured out how to transfer my camcorder footage onto my grandpa's computer and began my first youtube channel in 2008.
                        however, it was very annoying to transfer so i instead became interested in making movies on the computer itself!
                    </h3>
                    <br><h3 class="simple">
                        i usually did pivot stick figure and mario sprite animations, but once i learned how to use advancemap to mod pokemon emerald i immediately fell in love.

                    </h3>
