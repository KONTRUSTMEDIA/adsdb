<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Why_Youll_Love_These_Pancakes"></span>Why You’ll Love These Pancakes<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>These homemade chocolate chip pancakes are better than anything you can get from a box mix. They’re light, fluffy, and filled with melted chocolate in every bite. The best part? You can make them in minutes using simple ingredients you probably already have in your kitchen! Plus, they’re easy to customize, so the whole family can enjoy their favorite toppings.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Ingredients_Youll_Need"></span>Ingredients You’ll Need:<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Flour</strong> – Regular all-purpose flour works perfectly for this recipe.</li>



<li><strong>Sugar</strong> – White sugar or brown sugar, whichever you prefer.</li>



<li><strong>Baking Powder</strong> – This helps the pancakes rise, making them soft and fluffy.</li>



<li><strong>Milk</strong> – Whole milk is best, but 2% works just fine.</li>



<li><strong>Eggs</strong> – Eggs help bind the batter and give the pancakes richness.</li>



<li><strong>Butter</strong> – Unsalted butter for the batter and cooking.</li>



<li><strong>Vanilla Extract &amp; Salt</strong> – These add flavor and depth to your pancakes.</li>



<li><strong>Chocolate Chips</strong> – Semi-sweet chocolate chips are my go-to, but feel free to use milk or dark chocolate chips for different sweetness levels.</li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_Make_Chocolate_Chip_Pancakes"></span>How to Make Chocolate Chip Pancakes<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Mix Dry Ingredients:</strong> In a large bowl, take and whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.</li>



<li><strong>Mix Wet Ingredients:</strong> In another bowl, whisk the milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract until fully combined.</li>



<li><strong>Combine Wet &amp; Dry Ingredients:</strong> Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. It’s okay if the batter is a little lumpy—don’t over-mix it!</li>



<li><strong>Add Chocolate Chips:</strong> Stir in the chocolate chips, making sure they’re spread throughout the batter.</li>



<li><strong>Cook the Pancakes:</strong> Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter. Scoop ¼ cup of batter into the frying pan for each pancake. Cook for about 3 minutes until bubbles form on top and the edges look set. Flip carefully and cook for another 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Keep pancakes warm as you cook the rest.</li>



<li><strong>Serve &amp; Enjoy:</strong> Stack the pancakes on a plate, drizzle with syrup, and add a pat of butter. Serve with your favorite toppings!</li>
</ol>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Add_Your_Favorite_Toppings"></span>Add Your Favorite Toppings<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>While these pancakes are perfect with just butter and syrup, there are so many fun ways to make them your own. Here are some ideas:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Fresh fruit:</strong> Add sliced bananas, berries, or strawberries for a burst of freshness.</li>



<li><strong>Whipped cream:</strong> For extra indulgence, top your pancakes with a dollop of whipped cream.</li>



<li><strong>Nutty crunch:</strong> Try adding crushed nuts like walnuts or pecans for a little crunch.</li>



<li><strong>Chocolate drizzle:</strong> If you love extra chocolate, drizzle some melted chocolate over your pancakes.</li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Perfect_Pancake_Tips_Some_Tips_For_Best_Result"></span>Perfect Pancake Tips: Some Tips For Best Result<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Don’t over-mix the batter:</strong> A few lumps are totally fine! Over-mixing will lead to tough pancakes, and nobody wants that.</li>



<li><strong>Choose your chocolate wisely:</strong> Semi-sweet chocolate chips are the best for balancing sweetness with the maple syrup. If you like sweeter pancakes, try milk chocolate chips. For a more intense flavor, go for dark chocolate chips.</li>



<li><strong>Use a measuring cup:</strong> To make sure all your pancakes are the same size, use a ¼ cup measuring cup to pour the batter.</li>
</ul>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Make_It_Your_Own_Fun_Variations"></span>Make It Your Own: Fun Variations<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>

<li><strong>Mini marshmallows:</strong> Make a “s’mores” version by tossing mini marshmallows in with the chocolate chips.</li>



<li><strong>Peanut butter swirl:</strong> Stir in a little peanut butter to the batter for a sweet and nutty flavor.</li>



<li><strong>Banana chocolate chip pancakes:</strong> Add mashed bananas to the batter for extra flavor and texture.</li>
</ul>
