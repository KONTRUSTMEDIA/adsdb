<p>I ULNA WANNA BE WITH YOU<br />
&#8230;<br />
I don&#8217;t know what it is that makes you feel so rad,<br />
You touched my elbow once and now my arm&#8217;s gone mad.<br />
You struck my nerve and made me squeal,<br />
It tingled down from skull to heel<br />
And now I can’t stop smiling, it’s true,<br />
&#8216;Cause I ulna wanna be with you!<br />
&#8230;<br />
I ulna wanna be with you,<br />
Even when my bones feel blue.<br />
You crack me up in every way,<br />
Like joints that pop but never stray.<br />
You’re the one my marrow knew—<br />
I ulna wanna be with you!</p>
