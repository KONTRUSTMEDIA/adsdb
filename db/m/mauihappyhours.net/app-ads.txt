<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center" id="what-is-a-happy-hour"><strong>What is a Happy Hour?</strong></h2>



<p class="has-text-align-center">Happy hour is a term for a period of time, usually in the afternoon or evening, in which a restaurant or bar serves alcoholic drinks, appetizers, and other food items at a reduced price. Sometimes “happy hour” is only one hour, but usually, it is 2-3 hours in duration, with specific times varying from restaurant to restaurant or bar to bar. Some restaurants and bars will have more than one happy hour in a day, such as an afternoon happy hour and a late night happy hour. Maui Happy Hours connects you to happy hours happening on Maui, as well as other great deals from local stores, lodging providers, activity companies, and more.&nbsp;</p>
