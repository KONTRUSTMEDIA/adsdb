<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The last number drawn in the lottery is called the &#8220;Bonus Ball&#8221; or &#8220;Booster Ball.&#8221; You pick a number between 1 and 49. If your number matches the Bonus Ball, you can win 45 times the amount you bet. This makes the Bonus Ball a big part of the game, offering a chance for a much bigger win.</span></p>
<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="UK_49s_Hot_and_Cold_numbers_balls"></span>UK 49s Hot and Cold numbers balls<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hot and cold numbers are numbers that have been drawn either a lot or very little in the UK 49s lottery. By looking at past draws, you can find out which numbers are &#8220;hot&#8221; (drawn often) and which are &#8220;cold&#8221; (drawn less often). </span><b>UK 49s Hot and Cold numbers</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> can help you guess which numbers might come up next.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Using </span><b>UK49s Hot and Cold Numbers</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is a popular way to try to predict the draw. But remember, this method isn&#8217;t always right. Just because a number was drawn a lot or a little before doesn&#8217;t mean it will be the same in the future. There&#8217;s always some luck and chance involved in the lottery.</span></p>
