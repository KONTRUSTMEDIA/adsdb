			    <h1 >Civils</h1>

          	       <div class="clearfix"></div>
<p>We offer full structural concrete packages, including detailed earthworks, reinforcement fixing, formwork and concrete placement through our wholly owned civil construction business <strong>Melchor</strong>. </p>
<p><span class="ui-provider a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z ab ac ae af ag ah ai aj ak">With a dedicated team of industry-recognised experts, Melchor brings innovative methods to deliver large and complex industrial and infrastructure projects in remote, regional and Perth-metropolitan locations. Melchor’s services can be utilised independently or as part of a comprehensive and vertically integrated solution.  </span></p>
<p>Services include:</p>
<ul>
<li>complex concrete structures</li>
<li>concrete placement and finishing</li>
<li>elevated and suspended formwork </li>
<li>detailed earthworks</li>
<li>tilt panel and noise wall works</li>
<li>temporary works</li>
<li>precast concrete</li>
<li>reinforcement prefabrication</li>
<li>reinforcement fixing</li>
<li>MSE walls</li>
<li>buried services</li>
<li>concrete remediation</li>
<li>waterproofing</li>
<li>electrical and instrumentation.</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
