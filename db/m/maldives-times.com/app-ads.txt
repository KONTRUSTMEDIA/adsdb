	<p><b>Welcome to Paradise: Discover the Maldives</b></p><p>What do you think of when you think of the “tropics”? White sandy beaches lined with palm trees and turquoise blue waters that are oh so clear! Unwind in absolute serenity, while basking in the sun, all the while enjoying the soothing rhythm of the ocean waves crashing into the beach sand. Almost all the resorts in the Maldives have extremely instagrammable overwater villas, private pools, direct access to lagoons and panoramic ocean views. The islands are truly an indulgent escape into utmost luxury.</p><p>If you’re an adrenaline junkie, there are an overwhelming number of activities for you to try out! More points if you’re fond of the water - obviously! It’s an archipelago of over a thousand coral islands that form 26 atolls! Try snorkelling to see the most vibrant coral reefs or diving with manta rays, sea turtles or even whale sharks if you’re lucky. For those to keep their heads over water, not to fret, plenty of options like paddle-boarding, surfing and parasailing await you in the Maldives.</p><p>Maldives is the absolute best place to honeymoon for newlyweds (no less if you’ve been married for a while). A couple’s oasis, a dreamy hideaway for two, that”s the Maldives for you! Picture yourself lazily strolling on a secluded beach with the sun at the horizon being the only difference between the ocean and the sky, a pink and purple sky, with only the sounds of the ocean waves crashing on the white sand being the interlude between you and your partner. Turn this into a private and intimate dinner under the stars. In moments like these, your love grows, with the intimacy of the silences and memories.</p><p>If the last paragraph made you think that the Maldivian islands are perfect only for couples, allow yourself to be disillusioned. Resorts and hotels offer a range of activities for the young ones! From highly interactive programs created and guided by marine biologists that nurture the inherent curiosity of children, to water sports designed especially for the little adventurers with highly trained activity leaders! You, as parents, can truly relax knowing that your children are exploring to their heart’s content and thoroughly enjoying themselves while experts are making sure they are safe and teaching them to keep this unique ecosystem safe.</p><p>Maldives is not just about beaches, spas and resorts or water sports. It has a rich cultural heritage waiting for you to catch a glimpse, or delve deep! Malé, the capital city has some great historical landmarks - the 17th century Hukuri Miskiy (Friday Mosque), the National Museum, home to ancient artifacts, royal relics and numerous cultural exhibits, the Malé fish market, where you can try the best local seafood and other delicacies indigenous to the region, fresh tropical fruits and traditional Maldivian cuisine, inspired by Indian, Sri Lankan and Arabic flavours.</p><p>Sustainability is a word we have all heard, but Maldives truly sets the benchmark. The country has a precious, delicate, and unique ecosystem to protect, and that is one of the many reasons why many resorts and organizations exclusively have eco-friendly practices, like using renewable energy, preserving and restoring the vivid coral reefs, they all promote responsible tourism. As a visitor, you too can do your part in helping by participating in the conservation efforts, so that years later, the place is just as beautiful, if not more, ensuring the country can continue to be a pristine paradise.</p><p>Whether you’re seeking tranquility, adventure, romance, or cultural discovery, the Maldives offers something for everyone. From its warm, welcoming people to its unmatched natural wonders, every moment spent in the Maldives is a celebration of beauty, relaxation, and joy. Step into this tropical paradise, and let the Maldives redefine your idea of a dream vacation.</p>
