          <p>While counting calories is the foundation of weight management, 2026 nutritional science focuses on Nutrient Density. This refers to the amount of vitamins, minerals, and high-quality protein you receive per calorie consumed.</p>
          <h3>The Role of Fiber in Satiety</h3>
          <p>One of the most overlooked aspects of the Qdoba menu is the fiber content found in the black and pinto beans. Fiber acts as a biological buffer, slowing the digestion of carbohydrates and preventing rapid insulin spikes.</p>
          <h3>Sodium-to-Potassium Ratios</h3>
          <p>To optimize heart health at Qdoba, pair your seasoned proteins with potassium-rich ingredients like Hand-Crafted Guacamole and fresh Pico de Gallo.</p>
          <h3>Protein Pacing for Muscle Synthesis</h3>
          <p>For fitness enthusiasts, hitting the leucine threshold is critical for muscle recovery. Most adults require approximately 30–40 grams of high-quality protein per meal.</p>
