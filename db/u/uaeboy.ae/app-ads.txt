<div class="entry-content single-content">
	
<p>In today&#8217;s <span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px;">world, almost 90 out of 100 people use WhatsApp. But do you know how long 1GB of Data is Used on WhatsApp? UAE has more expensive packages than other countries.
