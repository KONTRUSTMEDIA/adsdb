				<h2>Explore the World of Ancient Rome</h2>
                <div class="separator"></div>
				<br />
				<p>The greatest empire the earth has ever known is more than just a collection of facts and figures. It represents both the glorious achievement, and at times contemptible, behavior of mankind. In the matter of just a few centuries, <a href="https://www.unrv.com/empire/founding.php" style="font-weight:600;" title="The Founding of Rome">Rome grew from a very small village</a> in central Italy to the absolute dominant power of the entire Italian peninsula. In a few more centuries, the Roman Empire's might reached <a href="https://www.unrv.com/provinces/britannia.php" style="font-weight:600;" title="Roman Britain">as far north as Britain</a>, east to Persia, and in the south it encompassed the whole of Northern Africa.</p>
				<p>Rome's extraordinary achievements and the unparalleled string of <a href="https://www.unrv.com/people/index.php" style="font-weight:600;">influential people</a> shaped the whole of Europe and even the rest of the world. One super powered nation, encompassing thousands of cultures kept order, stability and civilization in an ancient world fraught with turmoil. <a href="https://www.unrv.com/empire.php" style="font-weight:600;">The fall of Roman Empire</a>, and the centuries that came to be known as the Dark Ages that followed illustrates the awesome responsibility, reach and impact of the Empire.</p>
