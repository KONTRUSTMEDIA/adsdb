(August 20, 2024)&#160;</strong>–&#160;The <a href="https://louisvillezoo.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Louisville Zoo</a> has welcomed a 1 ½-month-old infant male western lowland gorilla named Abeo (ah-bay-OH) to our award-winning Gorilla Forest habitat. Born on June 28 at the <a href="https://www.zoo.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Woodland Park Zoo</a> in Seattle, Washington., the infant is the offspring of Akenji, a first-time mom.
