<div class="small reveal cMain_ctl33-row-menu row-menu-reveal" id="cMain_ctl33_divmenu_1295" data-reveal><h4>Division Information</h4><p><span style="background-color: #1f497d; color: #17365d;">&nbsp;</span>All our divisions are open to both boys and girls, and the season begins in May with divisions 9-18U ending in August.</p>
<p>
<br />
All players&nbsp;will need a baseball glove, athletic&nbsp;(running) shoes or rubber cleats, and a drink.<br />
<br />
Game schedules will not be available for 9-18U divisions until the beginning of May.<br />
<br />
<strong>Little Sluggers (2021 &amp; 2022)</strong> <br />
<br />
Registration&nbsp;Fee is $75, which includes a jersey and baseball cap. The&nbsp;5&nbsp;sessions (1 hour each) are held on&nbsp;Saturday mornings&nbsp;starting May 23rd. The final session will be June 20th (Weather Permitting). <br />
There are 3 sessions - one starting at&nbsp;9am, one starting at&nbsp;10:15am, and one starting at&nbsp;11:30am.&nbsp;<br />
Generally, sessions are grouped by age, with the younger kids starting at 9am and the older kids starting at 11:30am.&nbsp;<br />
<br />
The program is designed for skill development for moving up to T-Ball and beyond.&nbsp; At each session your child will move through 5-6 stations where they will work on throwing, catching, base running, etc.Home field will be&nbsp;Mornington Park.<br />
<br />
If you have any questions, please contact the&nbsp;Little Sluggers Convenor Lou DaSilva l<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#05696c71716960766970626260777645606767642b6664"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="6e071a1a020b1d021b09090b1c1d2e0b0c0c0f400d0f">[email&#160;protected]</span></a><span style="color: #17365d;"><span class="mod"><span class="modBody" style="font-family: Arial;"><br />
<br />
<br />
<strong></strong></span></span><strong>T-Ball (2019 &amp; 2020)</strong>
</span></p>
Registration Fee is $125, which includes a jersey and baseball cap.&nbsp;<br />
<br />
The season is set to begin Saturday, May 16th and will run to&nbsp;Sunday July 12th (weather permitting).&nbsp;There will not be a session on July 1st.
<br />
<br />
All sessions will be held at&nbsp;Mornington&nbsp;Park.
Players will be placed in groups of 10 with 1 group per field. Groups will meet for 90 minutes every Saturday morning. <br />
<br />
Each group will rotate through practice stations to develop fundamental baseball skills. Emphasis is placed on learning baseball skills and utilizing them in game situations.
Parent participation is crucial to the success of the program. <br />
<br />
Coaches and assistants are needed.&nbsp;EBBA is a volunteer organization and without volunteers, we cannot run the program.&nbsp;&nbsp;Experience (baseball or coaching) is not necessary. Stations will be set up, and coaches and assistants will be supplied with detailed practice drills. <br />
<br />
If you have any questions, please contact the T-Ball Convenor Grant Peter <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#cabea8aba6a68aafa8a8abe4a9ab">&nbsp;</a><a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#0b7f696a67674b6e69696a25686a"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="027660636e6e42676060632c6163">[email&#160;protected]</span>&nbsp;</a><br />
<br />
<strong>9U (2017 &amp; 2018)</strong>
<br />
<br />
Registration Fee is $260, which includes a Jersey, pants, socks and a baseball cap. <br />
<br />
The season&nbsp;will commence the week of&nbsp;May 10 (tentatively) and will run until the week of July 27th with end of season playoffs running that week.
All games will be at Mornington Park, Diamond 2, 6:00-8:00 pm.
There will be 20-2-hour sessions, 2 per week.
<br />
<br />
This is an instructional and developmental program for beginners as well as seasoned players.&nbsp;Players are also taught how to "make plays" using simple teamwork skills. FUN,&nbsp;LEARNING&nbsp;and TEAMWORK are emphasized at this age. All players are encouraged to try every position on the field.&nbsp;&nbsp;Basic skills are developed &ndash; pitching, batting, running bases, catching and throwing.&nbsp; Players must provide their own batting helmets.<br />
<br />
Your player will also need (as well as the items listed above) a batting helmet, and protective cup&nbsp; <br />
<br />
<strong>9U In-door Clinics&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong>
<br />

We are offering our 2025/2026 registered 9U players to participate in a pre-season in-door clinic to help get them warmed up and start building their skills that they will continue to build on in the regular season.&nbsp; If a player did not register to play baseball in the previous season...they are required to register for the 2026 season before they can participate in these clinics.
Players&nbsp;should bring indoor shoes, water bottle, baseball glove and wear a protective cup.
<br />
<br />
In-door clinics will be starting the week of January 19th, 2026, from 6:00-8:00 pm at JP Robarts Public School.&nbsp; Stay informed for exact dates/times in the news section as well as our FaceBook page. <strong>Note</strong> that there will not be a clinic offered during March Break.
