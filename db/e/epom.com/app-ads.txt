                <h2 class="title">
                    FAQ
                </h2>
                <div class="faq__box">
                    <div class="faq__column">
                        <div class="faq__item">
                            <div class="faq__item-quest" tabindex="0">
                                What is adtech?
                            </div>
                            <div class="faq__item-answer">
                                <p>
                                    Adtech is short for advertising technology. It refers to the use of <a href="https://epom.com/blog/ad-server/ad-tech-101" target="_blank">technology
                                    in the advertising industry</a> to create, deliver, and manage ads as a service. Adtech utilized various tools, platforms, and software that enable businesses to create, manage, and optimize their ad campaigns. It helps advertisers reach their target audience more effectively, track the performance of their ads, and make data-driven decisions to improve their advertising efforts.
                                </p>
                            </div>
                        </div>
                        <div class="faq__item">
                            <div class="faq__item-quest" tabindex="0">
                                How does adtech work?
                            </div>
                            <div class="faq__item-answer">
                                <p>
                                    Adtech implies collection of user data with various tools like cookies, tracking pixels, and social media integration. This data includes info about user behavior, interests, demographics, and more.
                                </p>
                                <p>
                                    Once the data is collected, it is analyzed and segmented into different audience groups. Advertisers can then use it to create personalized ads that are more likely to resonate with their target audience.
                                </p>
                            </div>
                        </div>
                        <div class="faq__item">
                            <div class="faq__item-quest" tabindex="0">
                                What is an adtech company?
                            </div>
                            <div class="faq__item-answer">
