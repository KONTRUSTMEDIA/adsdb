    <p class="tag">“Health information brought to life.”</p>
    <p>eMedTV was an early pioneer in patient-friendly health video content. It was launched in 2007 by former US Navy physician Dr. Arthur Schoenstadt. eMedTV combined short, physician-narrated videos with rigorously edited articles.</p>

    <p>The site's library grew to more than 3,500 topics and was among the most visited health sites in the 2010s. The project was quietly retired in 2019.</p>
    <p>A full <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20191202034542/http://www.emedtv.com/" target="_blank">site archive</a> is available on Archive.org and some clips remain accessible on eMedTV's <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@emedtv" target="_blank">YouTube channel</a>.
