                      <li>Wood bats</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p><u><strong>Minors</strong></u></p>
                    <ul>
                      <li>USA bat - Barrel size no&nbsp; bigger than 2-5/8&quot;</li>
                      <li>Wood bats</li>
                    </ul>

                    <ul>
                      <li>USA bat - Barrel size no bigger than 2-5/8&quot;&nbsp;</li>
                      <li>BBCOR bats</li>
                      <li>Wood bats</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p><u>Connie Mack</u></p>
                    <ul>
                      <li>BBCOR only (Aluminum)</li>
                      <li>Wood bats</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>Note: If a player uses an illegal bat during play, a dead ball will be called and the batter will be called out.&nbsp; If after a play is completed, the bat is found to be illegal, the batter is out and the base runners will return to where they previously were.&nbsp; The opposing team would have until the next pitch is thrown to make a claim of an illegal bat.</p>
