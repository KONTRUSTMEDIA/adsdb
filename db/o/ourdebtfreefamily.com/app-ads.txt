<section id="custom_html-3" class="widget_text widget widget_custom_html"><div class="widget_text widget-wrap"><h4 class="widget-title widgettitle">Five Steps To Debt Freedom</h4>
<div class="textwidget custom-html-widget">Here are five simple guidlines that will help you pay off debt.
&nbsp;
<p></p>
<b>1) Get an emergency fund</b> so you don’t take on debt when something comes up. 
<p></p>
<b>2) List your debts.</b> This way you know where you stand.
<p></p>
<b>3) Use the debt snowball.</b> Pay your debts from smallest to largest, or most expensive to least expensive.
<p></p>
<b>4) Avoid new debt.</b> No new credit cards or loans. Period.
<p></p>
<b>5) Go all cash.</b> After everything is paid off, switch to all cash.</div></div></section>
