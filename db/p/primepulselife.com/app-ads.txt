<p>First off, let’s talk short hairstyles! Who says short hair can’t be glamorous? With the right styling, a short updo or a chic pixie cut can rival any lengthy mane in the beauty department. <p>Now, let’s weave in the beauty of medium length hair, which offers a fantastic canvas to work with for wedding hairstyles.
