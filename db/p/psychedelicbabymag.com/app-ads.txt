          <h2 class="sr-only">Dom Mariani Unveils Final Single &#8216;Breaking Point&#8217; Ahead of New Album &#8216;Apple of Life!&#8217;</h2>

          
<h1>Dom Mariani, the frontman of The Stems, DM3, and Datura4, returns to share his latest single, &#8216;Breaking Point,&#8217; from his upcoming solo album, out October 31 via Alive Naturalsound Records.</h1>
<hr />
<p>Recorded in Fremantle, Western Australia, this new album showcases Dom Mariani&#8217;s undiminished gift for melody and the classic power-pop style that powered his work with The Stems, DM3, and The Someloves.</p>
<p>The track &#8216;Breaking Point&#8217; is a standout, shimmering with the irresistible, hook-filled pleasures that define truly great pop music.</p>
<p>Despite its sunny production, the song’s origins are pure New York grit. Mariani notes, &#8220;The song had been floating around for a while with what started out as some lyrical ideas while on a subway train in New York a few years back.&#8221; He explains that the &#8220;sense of urgency and chaos I felt on that ride fueled the narrative,&#8221; which then fit seamlessly with the &#8220;tough guitar progression already in my head.&#8221;</p>
<p>The result is a pop revelation, born in the Big Apple&#8217;s tunnels and perfected on the Western Australian coast.</p>
