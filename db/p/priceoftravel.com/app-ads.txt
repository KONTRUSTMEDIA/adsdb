    <h2 class="intro__title mb-30">
        About    </h2>
   

    <div class="wrapper">
        <div class="w-70">
			<div class="page_thumbnail">
							</div>
			<div class="page__content">
				
<p>Price of Travel is a database of travel costs in every major destination around the world, started in early 2010. The site now features over 200 cities, with information for each listing accommodation, transportation, attraction, food, and drink prices.</p>



<p>We&#8217;ll be adding new features all the time that will allow visitors to figure out destinations and budgets that fit their own travel style, in ways that no other resource has done.</p>



<p><strong>Roger Wade</strong><br><em>founder/editor-in-chief</em></p>



<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="alignright"><img decoding="async" src="https://www.priceoftravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/RogerWadeAlps.jpg" alt="RogerWadeAlps" class="wp-image-6442"/></figure></div>



<p>With an earlier background in entertainment and print publishing, Los Angeles-native Roger Wade worked for several years as the Content Director and lead writer at the BootsnAll Travel Network, based in Portland, Oregon. During this time he wrote and contributed to a number of successful online travel guide sites for the network, and developed new products and features for a wide range of sites as well.</p>



<p>An urge to get back on the road led Roger to striking out on his own with Price of Travel, which is the first website that allows anyone from a backpacker to 5-star luxury traveler to make a daily budget at a glance for every major travel destination around the world.</p>



<p>Roger has traveled extensively all of his life, having been to 47 US states and nearly 90 countries in almost every corner of the world. He has also lived in a long list of cities in Europe and Asia, in addition to many different locations all over the United States.</p>
