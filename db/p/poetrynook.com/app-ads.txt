            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p>In my dream, I fell through the floor,<br>Whispers of a father I can’t ignore.<br>His hands were warm, but his eyes were cold,<br>Behind that mask, a truth untold.</p><p>I reached for him, but he slipped away,<br>A shadow where his love should stay.<br>A laugh that shattered, sharp and cruel,<br>The mask of love, a twisted fool.</p><p>Am I alive, or just a ghost he made?<br>I can’t recall the promises he betrayed.<br>All that’s left is the hollow air,<br>But the mask? Oh, it lingers there.</p></div>
