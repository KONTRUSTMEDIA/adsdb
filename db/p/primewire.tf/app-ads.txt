<div class="post-content"><h1 id="primewire-nexus">PrimeWire Nexus<a hidden class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#primewire-nexus">#</a></h1>
<p>PrimeWire Nexus is the new informational hub for PrimeWire, providing information about the current status of PrimeWire and which domains you can use to access our service.</p>
<p>Any active domains (marked with ✅) can be used to access PrimeWire.  You can use the same account to log in to PrimeWire on any of the domains and all of your data will be there.  However, if you are logged in on one domain, you will not be automatically logged in on the other domains.  Never enter your PrimeWire credentials on a domain that is not marked with a ✅.</p>
<h2 id="current-status">Current Status<a hidden class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#current-status">#</a></h2>
<p>PrimeWire is online and in good condition.  You can now log in on the .mov/.si/.zip domains and all of your data will be there.</p>
