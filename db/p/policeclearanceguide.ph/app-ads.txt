	<h1 class="entry-title">National Police Clearance – Police Clearance Online Registration in the Philippines (2026)</h1></header><!-- .entry-header -->

<div class="entry-content single-content">
	
<p>Over the years, the police clearance application has become faster and more efficient due to the Philippine National Police (PNP), which activated the online system called the NPCS or National Police Clearance System. Moreover, as long as you have internet access and a device to apply for clearance, you won’t have to wait for long hours in the queue at the police station.&nbsp;</p><div class="polic-content-1" id="polic-3775800567">
<div id="container-9db0d6b59c2929054db32c19a64f8cc3"></div></div>



<p>The online system is a major benefit for job seekers, both locally and abroad. Many employers require a police clearance to confirm that applicants have no criminal record.</p>



<p>In this article, we’ll cover the most common issues people face when applying for police clearance online in the Philippines. We’ll also share practical solutions to help you avoid delays and make the process smoother. Whether you&#8217;re applying online or in person, this guide will give you everything you need to get your clearance without hassle.</p>

<h2 class="kt-adv-heading9_b64e9c-af wp-block-kadence-advancedheading has-theme-palette-3-color has-text-color" data-kb-block="kb-adv-heading9_b64e9c-af"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="What_Is_a_National_Police_Clearance"></span><strong>What Is a National Police Clearance?</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>


<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-full is-resized"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="948" height="592" src="https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/police-clearance-certificate.png" alt="National Police Clearance Certificate" class="wp-image-1152" style="width:655px;height:auto" srcset="https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/police-clearance-certificate.png 948w, https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/police-clearance-certificate-300x187.png 300w, https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/police-clearance-certificate-768x480.png 768w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 948px) 100vw, 948px" /><figcaption class="wp-element-caption"><em>Illustrative Example Only — This is a mock sample for educational purposes and not an official document.</em></figcaption></figure>
</div>


<p>A police clearance is issued by the Philippine National Police to certify whether a person has any criminal record. In simple words, police clearance means that the applicant has never committed any crime or has no criminal history or legal infractions. However, this document is important for employment purposes since many employers want to know whether applicants have had any records.</p>



<div class="polic-content" id="polic-2702387391"><script type="2ec03aeed970b981f529dbe4-text/javascript">

  };
</script>
</div><p>It’s often required when:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Applying for a job</li>



<li>Taking the civil service exam</li>



<li>Registering a firearm</li>



<li>Getting a credit card</li>



<li>Applying for government documents like a birth certificate</li>
</ul>



<p>In the Philippines, the National Police Clearance is also an accepted ID for some transactions like applications for a credit card, for national ID, or for NBI clearance. Please note that it is not valid for passport applications under the DFA or for voter registration with the COMELEC.</p>



<p>To fast-track the process, the Philippine National Police (PNP) instituted the National Police Clearance System (NPCS) as a digital system that unifies criminal records from different sources into one massive database. Through this system, applicants no longer have to come to the police station or even local offices to apply. Applicants can now apply online from the comfort of their homes using a computer and internet connection. A swift and easy way to obtain such clearances!</p><div class="polic-leadeboard" id="polic-2622472684"><script type="2ec03aeed970b981f529dbe4-text/javascript">

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_Apply_for_National_Police_Clearance_Online"></span><strong>How to Apply for National Police Clearance Online</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Applying for a police clearance online in the Philippines is quick and convenient. </p>



<p>Just follow these steps:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_Apply_for_Local_Police_Clearance"></span><strong>How to Apply for Local Police Clearance</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>



<p>Local police clearance is usually very convenient and hassle-free, especially for those with no court cases pending. </p>



<p>Here are the steps involved:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">

<li>You’ll be given a Police Clearance Request Form. Fill it out completely and attach any required documents.</li>

<li>Once everything is checked, local police clearance will be done in a day.</li>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Tips_and_Warnings_for_Getting_a_Police_Clearance_Online"></span><strong>Tips and Warnings for Getting a Police Clearance Online</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>


<div class="wp-block-image">
<figure class="aligncenter size-full is-resized"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="863" height="770" src="https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Tips-and-Warnings-for-Getting-a-Police-Clearance-Online.png" alt="Tips and Warnings for Getting a Police Clearance Online" class="wp-image-1159" style="width:692px;height:auto" srcset="https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Tips-and-Warnings-for-Getting-a-Police-Clearance-Online.png 863w, https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Tips-and-Warnings-for-Getting-a-Police-Clearance-Online-300x268.png 300w, https://policeclearanceguide.ph/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Tips-and-Warnings-for-Getting-a-Police-Clearance-Online-768x685.png 768w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 863px) 100vw, 863px" /></figure>
</div>


<p><strong>1. Missed your appointment? You still have time.</strong></p>



<p>If you don’t show up on your scheduled date, don’t worry—you still have 15 days to visit the police station you selected and complete the process. But if you go beyond that 15-day window, your payment will be forfeited and you’ll have to apply again.</p>



<p>For example, one applicant missed her appointment due to a lockdown. She was able to reschedule her appointment online without paying again. So it’s worth checking your account if you need to reschedule.</p>



<p><strong>2. You can’t change personal info after printing.</strong></p>



<p>Once your police clearance is printed, you can’t edit any of the details. If you made a mistake, you’ll need to apply again, set a new appointment, and pay the fee. Also, you&#8217;ll need to visit the same police station for biometrics.</p>



<p><strong>3. You can verify your clearance online.</strong></p>



<p>Want to check if your police clearance is legit? Go to the NPCS website and click “Verify Clearance.”<br>Enter your surname and clearance number, complete the CAPTCHA, and click &#8220;Verify.&#8221; It’s that simple.</p>



<p><strong>4. Appointments are only available on weekdays.</strong></p>



<p>Police stations only process clearances from Monday to Friday. No slots are available on weekends. You can choose between morning and afternoon slots, so try to pick a schedule that works best with your day off.</p>



<p><strong>5. Police clearance can’t be renewed.</strong></p>



<p>Unlike the NBI clearance, police clearance doesn’t have a renewal option. Once it expires—after 6 months—you’ll need to apply again from scratch.</p>

<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-1 kt-pane9_35d0fb-d0"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">How can I use my police clearance for work or immigration abroad?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>To use your police clearance internationally, get the original copy from the PNP and have it Apostillized (this replaced the old “red ribbon” process since June 17, 2019). You’ll need to follow the latest DFA procedure to do this.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-2 kt-pane9_d1dc91-50"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">What’s the difference between NBI clearance and police clearance?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>Here’s a quick comparison:</p>



<figure class="wp-block-table"><table class="has-fixed-layout"><tbody><tr><td></td><td>NBI Clearance</td><td>Police Clearance</td></tr><tr><td>Database</td><td>National-level (court records)</td><td>Now also national via NPCS</td></tr><tr><td>Before NPCS</td><td>Covered all regions</td><td>Local station only</td></tr><tr><td>After NPCS (2018)</td><td>Still broader legally</td><td>Now centralized and accessible nationwide</td></tr><tr><td>HIT processing</td><td>Can take up to 10 days</td><td>Often resolved faster, especially if it&#8217;s just a namesake</td></tr><tr><td>Use case</td><td>Passport, immigration, employment</td><td>Employment, exams, permits, etc.</td></tr></tbody></table></figure>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-3 kt-pane9_be15d7-f2"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">I&#8217;m a Filipino/foreigner living abroad. How can I get a police clearance from the Philippines?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>If you are living abroad and need to obtain a police clearance from the Philippines, what you really want is an NBI Clearance is the equivalent of a police clearance certificate in other nations.</p>



<p>This document mentions all sorts of records, such as acquitted cases and traffic violations that went to court. It is mostly needed when applying for visas, immigration, jobs, or residency in other countries.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-4 kt-pane9_fd3836-5b"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">Do you need one or two police clearances?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>It depends on what the country you&#8217;re applying to asks for:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Some countries, like the United States, require police certificates from both:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Your current country of residence (if you’ve lived there more than 6 months)</li>



<li>Your country of nationality (if different from your current residence)</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<p>Example: If you’re an OFW in Saudi Arabia applying for a U.S. immigrant visa, you’ll need:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>A police certificate from Saudi Arabia</li>



<li>An NBI Clearance from the Philippines</li>
</ul>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_apply_for_NBI_Clearance_abroad"></span>How to apply for NBI Clearance abroad<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<p>You have two options:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Apply through an authorized representative in the Philippines.</li>



<li>Apply on your own via mail through the Philippine Embassy/Consulate.</li>
</ol>



<p><strong>To apply from abroad, follow these steps:</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Get an NBI Fingerprint Card Form No. 5. You can get this from the nearest Philippine embassy or consulate.</li>



<li>Fill out the form and have your fingerprints taken by the embassy or local police authorities.</li>



<li>Prepare the required documents:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Completed NBI Form No. 5</li>



<li>2&#215;2 ID photo with white background (taken within the last 3 months)</li>



<li>Valid passport photocopy</li>



<li>Any proof of your purpose (like an immigration or visa letter)<br></li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Send everything to the NBI Clearance Division in Manila, or hand it over to your authorized representative in the Philippines.</li>
</ul>



<p>You’ll receive your clearance via international mail or through your representative.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-5 kt-pane9_3f1f23-97"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">How can I pay the police clearance via 7-Eleven?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>If you don’t have a bank account or just want a quick and easy payment option, 7-Eleven’s CLiQQ app is a convenient way to pay your police clearance fee.</p>



<p>Here’s how to do it:</p>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Step-by-step_guide_to_paying_via_7-Eleven"></span><strong>Step-by-step guide to paying via 7-Eleven:</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Log in to your NPCS account<br>Go to the<a href="https://pnpclearance.ph/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> PNP clearance site</a> and log in with your registered email and password.<br></li>



<li>Go to Transaction History<br>Click on <em>Transaction History</em> from the menu.<br></li>



<li>Click “Click here to pay”<br>Don’t worry about the “Over the counter not allowed” warning—it only applies to police stations. You can still pay via 7-Eleven.<br></li>



<li>Choose “Cash Payment” as your payment option<br>On the LandBank ePayment portal, select <em>Cash Payment</em> and click <em>Submit</em>.<br></li>



<li>Review the transaction details<br>Confirm the amount:<br>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Police Clearance Fee: ₱150</li>



<li>Convenience Fee: ₱30</li>



<li>Total: ₱180<br></li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Tick the Terms and Conditions box, then click <em>Submit</em>.<br></li>



<li>Select “7-Eleven” as your payment channel<br>You’ll be redirected to the MyEG Philippines gateway. Choose <em>7-Eleven</em>, review the payment deadline, and click <em>Proceed</em>.<br></li>



<li>Copy and save your payment reference number<br>A 10-character alphanumeric code will appear. Screenshot or write this down—this is what you’ll use at 7-Eleven.<br></li>



<li>Download and install the CLiQQ app<br>Find it on the App Store or Google Play.<br></li>



<li>Create an account on CLiQQ<br>Register using your mobile number or Google/Facebook account.<br></li>



<li>Select “Pay Bills”<br>In the app dashboard, tap <em>Pay Bills</em>.<br></li>



<li>Choose “Government” as the biller category<br>Now select <em>MYEGPH</em> from the list.<br></li>



<li>Enter the payment reference number<br>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Payment Ref No. (from step 7)</li>



<li>Mobile Number</li>



<li>Amount: ₱180<br>Click <em>Confirm</em>.<br></li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>Go to any 7-Eleven store<br>In the app, go to <em>Manage Transactions → Bills Payment</em> to generate a barcode.<br></li>



<li>Present the barcode to the cashier<br>Pay the amount and keep the receipt. This is proof of payment.</li>
</ol>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="On_the_day_of_your_appointment_bring"></span>On the day of your appointment, bring:<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h4>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The 7-Eleven receipt</li>



<li>2 valid IDs</li>



<li>The email address used to book your appointment</li>



<li>The reference number from step 3 (different from the 7-Eleven payment code)</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-6 kt-pane9_48e35f-10"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">How long is the Police Clearance valid?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>The clearance issued by the National Police serves as evidence or verification of character for no more than six (6) months from the date of issuance.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-7 kt-pane9_559305-15"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">Can I use my police clearance for multiple purposes?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>Sure, but if an institution requests clearance for a different purpose, the application would need to be lodged again with the correct purpose stated in the application.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-8 kt-pane9_7c68ad-53"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">Can I reschedule my appointment?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>Yes, but only before payment. After payment is made, rescheduling is not allowed.</p>
</div></div></div>



<div class="wp-block-kadence-pane kt-accordion-pane kt-accordion-pane-9 kt-pane9_2cab13-b1"><div class="kt-accordion-header-wrap"><button class="kt-blocks-accordion-header kt-acccordion-button-label-show" type="button"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title-wrap"><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-title">What if I miss my appointment?</span></span><span class="kt-blocks-accordion-icon-trigger"></span></button></div><div class="kt-accordion-panel kt-accordion-panel-hidden"><div class="kt-accordion-panel-inner">
<p>If you miss your schedule, you’ll need to reapply and pay again.</p>
</div></div></div>
</div></div></div>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="Conclusion"></span><strong>Conclusion</strong><span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>



<p>Applying for a National Police Clearance Online in the Philippines is now more efficient and accessible. With a streamlined process through <a href="https://pnpclearance.ph" target="_blank" rel="noopener">pnpclearance.ph</a>, citizens can easily fulfill this legal requirement without long queues or unnecessary paperwork. Stay updated, verify your information, and follow this guide for a seamless experience.</p>
