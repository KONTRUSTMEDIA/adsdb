	<div class="entry-content mh-clearfix">
		<p>Hello!</p>
<p>Welcome to my website.</p>
<p>With this website I hope to provide information regarding weak signal communication in ham radio. It reports about my activity over the last 30 years. I hope you find this information usefull.</p>
<p>The information provided here shows some of my projects.&nbsp; Don&#8217;t look for complete building descriptions, these donnot exist in the PA3CSG shack. Projects are a sort&nbsp; &#8220;one of&#8221;. Only one item is build and it was for my own needs. All projects on this website are in a working state otherwise I would not publish them. .</p>
<p>My good friend and helper, Jack PE1KXH also shows some of his projects on these pages.</p>
<p>Enjoy!</p>
<p>Geert PA3CSG</p>
