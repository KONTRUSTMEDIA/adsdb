</article>
<article class="news-box">
<p>Cozy abstract adventure 
<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/4367920/What_Surrounds_Us_Demo/" target="_blank">What Surrounds Us</a> 
demo launches for Steam Next Fest 2026, offering the first 15 minutes to explore an ecosystem 
as a creature solving environmental puzzles by observing flora and fauna.<br><br>
Players interact with surroundings to restore balance against an unknown threat in this relaxing, 
non-moralizing ecological experience inspired by In Other Waters, A Short Hike, and Neva.<br><br>
The full game is planned for PC release in Q2 2026.
