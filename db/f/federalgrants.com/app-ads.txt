<p>The FederalGrants.com mission is to inform the general public about federal grants offered by the United States government.
We strive to provide accurate information on how to find and qualify for a grant, how to apply for a grant, and how to proceed once you are approved.
The Federal Grant search engine allows access to the requirements, deadlines, and details of over 60,000 past and current grants from 500+ different federal agencies.</p>

<div class="col2">
<b>What are Federal Grants?</b><br>
There is a multitude of misinformation and misunderstanding about federal
grants on the internet and on television.&nbsp; It is extremely important to
fully understand what government grants are before you spend valuable time and money
searching for grant sources or looking for a grant writer. <a href="/what-is-a-grant.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/federal-grants-for-women.html">
<img border="0" src="images/grants-for-women.png" alt="Federal Grants for Women" width="50" height="50"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>Federal Grants for Women</b><br>
Simply being a woman doesn't ensure you will qualify for a federal grant.&nbsp;
There are many more qualifications needed, including having a business or non-profit
organization geared towards helping women and an established track record of
service.
<a href="/federal-grants-for-women.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/federal-housing-grants.html">
<img border="0" src="images/housing-grants.png" alt="Federal Housing Grants" width="50" height="43"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>Federal Housing Grants</b><br>
Up to $1 billion in housing grants are available through HUD.&nbsp; Communities
in need of help to spur economic growth and development may qualify for HUD
grants.&nbsp; Individuals are typically not granted assistance, however help
may be available through local government and organizations. <a href="/federal-housing-grants.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/small-business-federal-grants.html">
<img border="0" src="images/small-business-grants.png" alt="Federal Grants for Small Businesses" width="50" height="44"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>Federal Grants for Small Businesses</b><br>
The U.S. Government does not generally award federal grants to private
for-profit small businesses.&nbsp; Typically only non-profit agencies benefit from
these grants.&nbsp; There are some exceptions, so researching thoroughly what
options are available is the first step towards securing a grant for your
small business.
<a href="/small-business-federal-grants.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/federal-pell-grants.html">
<img border="0" src="images/pell-grants.png" alt="Federal Pell Grant" width="50" height="50"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>Federal Pell Grant</b><br>
Pell Grants are awarded directly to college students who meet the established
criteria.&nbsp; The purpose of the Federal Pell Grant program is to promote
access to postsecondary education for low-income citizens.&nbsp; The amount
awarded is based on the individual's financial need.
<a href="/federal-pell-grants.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/apply-for-a-grant.html">
<img border="0" src="images/apply-grants.png" alt="How to Apply for a Federal Grant" width="50" height="50"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>How to Apply for a Federal Grant</b><br>
Applying for a federal grant can often be the most daunting task of all.&nbsp;
Before beginning this process many steps are necessary, including ensuring
your readiness, making sure you meet all of the qualifications, and planning
the writing process.
<a href="/apply-for-a-grant.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/grant-writers.html">
<img border="0" src="images/grant-writers.png" alt="How to Find, Evaluate, and Hire a Grant Writer" width="50" height="48"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>How to Find, Evaluate, and Hire a Grant Writer</b><br>
Writing your grant request takes not only practice but knowledge of the
process.&nbsp; Often times finding a grant writer is more beneficial to
organizations than attempting to write the grant yourself.&nbsp; Referrals,
references, and research are all important steps to ensuring you find a capable
grant writer.
<a href="/grant-writers.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="row">
<div class="col1">
<a href="/free-federal-grants.html">
<img border="0" src="images/free-federal-grants.png" alt="The Truth About Free Federal Grants" width="50" height="50"></a>
</div>
<div class="col2">
<b>The Truth About Free Federal Grants</b><br>
We debunk the "free grant" myth you hear on late night TV commericals.&nbsp;
The truth about free federal grants is there are no free federal grants!&nbsp;
The United States government doesn't give away free grant money for individuals
to buy a home or pay bills,so don't worry, you're not missing out.
<a href="/free-federal-grants.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="col2">
<b>Real World Grant Writing Example</b><br>
With so many different types of government grants, each with varying requirements and
processes, a real-world grant writing example is helpful.&nbsp; To aid you
in the process, we explore the nine most common sections in a well-written
grant proposal. You'll be well on your way in applying for your grant.
<a href="/grant-writing.html">Read More &gt;&gt;</a>
