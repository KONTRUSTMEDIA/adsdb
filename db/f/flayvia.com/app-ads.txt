<p></p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Essential Ingredients for Dutch Apple Bread</h2>



<p></p>



<p><strong>Green Apple</strong> – Diced and peeled, the apples provide natural sweetness and a slight tartness that balances the richness of the bread.</p>



<p><strong>Butter</strong> – Adds moisture and flavor, making the bread tender and rich.</p>



<p><strong>Sugar (Granulated &amp; Brown)</strong> – Sweetens the batter and enhances the crumb topping for a delicious caramelized crust.</p>



<p><strong>Cinnamon</strong> – Brings warmth and spice, elevating the flavors of the apple and topping.</p>



<p><strong>Eggs</strong> – Bind the ingredients together and help the bread rise, giving it structure and lightness.</p>



<p><strong>All-Purpose Flour</strong> – The base for the batter, providing structure and substance to the bread.</p>



<p><strong>Nuts (Walnuts or Pecans)</strong> – Add a crunchy texture and earthy flavor, complementing the apples beautifully.</p>



<p><strong>Vanilla Extract</strong> – Enhances the flavor profile, giving a delightful depth to both the batter and the glaze.</p>



<p><strong>Baking Powder</strong> – Ensures the bread rises properly, resulting in a light, fluffy texture.</p>



<p></p>



<figure class="wp-block-image alignwide size-full"><img data-recalc-dims="1" decoding="async" width="600" height="400" src="https://i0.wp.com/flayvia.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/da2.png?resize=600%2C400" alt="" class="wp-image-398" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/flayvia.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/da2.png?w=600&amp;ssl=1 600w, https://i0.wp.com/flayvia.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/da2.png?resize=300%2C200&amp;ssl=1 300w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></figure>



<p></p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Simple Steps to Make Your Dutch Apple Bread</h2>



<p></p>



<p>Preheat your oven to 350°F and prepare your bread pan with parchment paper or a light coating of non-stick spray.</p>



<p>Chop fresh apples into small squares and set them aside.</p>



<p>In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Mix in the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.</p>



<p>Gradually add the flour, salt, and baking powder, mixing just until the ingredients are incorporated. Fold in the diced apples and nuts.</p>



<p>Pour the batter into the prepared pan and set it aside while you prepare the Dutch crumb topping.</p>



<p>To make the topping, use a fork or pastry blender to combine cold butter, both sugars, flour, and cinnamon until crumbly. Evenly sprinkle the mixture over the batter in the pan.</p>



<p>Bake the bread for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool before removing it from the pan.</p>



<p>While the bread cools, whisk together melted butter, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth to make the glaze. Drizzle over the cooled bread, slice, and enjoy!</p>



<p></p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Recipe Tips for Perfect Dutch Apple Bread</h2>



<p></p>



<p>For a more pronounced apple flavor, use tart green apples like Granny Smith.</p>



<p>Ensure your butter is cold when making the crumb topping; this helps achieve a crumbly texture.</p>



<p>Allow the bread to cool completely before adding the glaze to prevent it from melting into the bread.</p>

<p></p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How to Store Your Dutch Apple Bread</h2>



<p></p>



<p>To keep your Dutch Apple Bread fresh, store it in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days. If you want to enjoy it for a longer period, refrigerate it for up to a week. </p>



<p></p>



<p>For extended storage, wrap the bread tightly in plastic wrap and place it in a freezer-safe bag before freezing. </p>



<p></p>



<p>To enjoy, thaw the bread at room temperature and reheat in the oven or microwave for a warm, freshly baked taste.</p>

<p>Yes, this bread freezes well. Allow the bread to cool completely, wrap it tightly in plastic wrap or aluminum foil, and then place it in an airtight container or freezer bag for up to 3 months. Thaw it at room temperature when ready to eat.</p>
