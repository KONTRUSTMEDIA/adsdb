                <p>The market capitalization of a company, also known as its market cap, is the total value of all outstanding shares of the company's stock as determined by the current market price of those shares. It is calculated by multiplying the total number of shares outstanding by the current market price of one share.</p>

It is also used as a metric for evaluating the performance of a company over time, as changes in market capitalization can indicate changes in investor sentiment about the company's prospects.</p><br>
