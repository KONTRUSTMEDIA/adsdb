<article class="cc-blogpost"><div class="cc-blogtitle"><a href="https://www.kiwiblitz.com/blog/status-of-the-comic-and-my-life">Status of the Comic (and my life)</a></div><div class="cc-blog-publishtime">Posted September 5, 2021 at 09:42 pm</div><div class="cc-blogcontent"><p>H--Heya everybody! As of today, September 5th, KB has its first new page in... over a year! Jeez!</p><p>So the long and short of it is that I can't yet promise a regular schedule for KB, but I hope to make pages whenever I'm in the right headspace and have time to do so.</p><p>The situation that caused the original hiatus (moving down to help my family as my dad deals with his ALS) hasn't exactly changed for the better or anything. I go over there every day, and finding the right balance between helping out there, drawing comics, and everything else is a whole lot. Sometimes it's just really tough to focus on work when I know I'm actively running out of time to spend with dad and help get all his affairs in order. I won't belabor that whole situation though. It is how it is! I promise I'm doing my best to take care of myself given the circumstances.<br></p><p>I just wanted to give a heads up as to where things are at. If you want to know when new KB pages appear from the mysterious ether, you can always follow the RSS or <b><a href="https://twitter.com/cubewatermelon" target="_blank">my twitter</a></b>!</p><p><b><a href="https://www.sleeplessdomain.com/" target="_blank">Sleepless Domain</a></b> is also still updating regularly, as is my most important work of <b><a href="https://twitter.com/CagleCats" target="_blank">Cat Picture Tweets</a></b>.</p><p>Thank you so much to everyone for your constant patience and understanding, and I hope I can draw more of these loser babies soon! I still love them very much.<br></p></div></article><div style="clear:both;"></div><div class="cc-readmore"><a href="https://www.kiwiblitz.com/blog"></a></div>    </div>
