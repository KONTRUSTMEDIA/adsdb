<p>Yahoo Weather is a free weather app available for both iPhones and Android phones that provides local and non-local weather information with daily and hourly forecasts overlaid on top of beautiful, hi-res photos using Yahoo&#8217;s own image service, Flickr. This guide will teach you how to use Yahoo Weather app on any mobile platform step-by-step.</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_under_page_title - under_page_title --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-102" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="under_page_title"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(102); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_under_page_title - under_page_title --><p><em>*This post may contain affiliate links. <h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_Use_Yahoo_Weather_App_on_Android"></span>How to Use&#xA0;Yahoo Weather App on Android<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
<p>Setting up your Yahoo Weather App is easy. Scroll down to see directions for Android users.</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_under_first_paragraph - under_first_paragraph --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-110" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="under_first_paragraph"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(110); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_under_first_paragraph - under_first_paragraph -->
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">1. Use Google Play Store and search for &#8220;<strong>Yahoo Weather</strong>&#8220;, or download it by using this link: <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.yahoo.mobile.client.android.weather&amp;hl=en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.yahoo.mobile.client.android.weather&amp;hl=en</a><br />
2. Once installed, press <strong>Allow</strong> to give permission for Yahoo Weather to access your device&#8217;s location for collecting&#xA0;weather information automatically.<br />
3. Next, Choose <strong>Enable</strong> or <strong>Disable</strong> to show daily weather information via <strong>notifications</strong> on your smartphone.<br />
4. Yahoo Weather allows weather monitoring of <strong>several locations at once</strong>. Choose any of the provided locations from all over the world by <strong>swiping&#xA0;to the right</strong> and tapping on each location you want to add. Now you&#8217;re ready to see the weather data.<br />
5. The main window <strong>displays</strong> the <strong>temperature on the bottom left</strong> and <strong>the city and time at the top</strong>.<br />
6. Tap on the plus sign in the top right corner to <strong>add any location</strong> not available from the choices before by entering the <strong>city or zip code</strong>.<br />
7. Scroll to the left on the main window <strong>to switch between locations</strong>, providing new photos and detailed weather information for each location chosen in the above step.<br />
8. Slide the screen up on the main window to see <strong>hourly</strong>, <strong>5 and 10-day weather forecasts</strong>, along with other detailed information such as: a local weather map, UV index, humidity, wind speeds and pressure, precipitation, sun and moon information, and sunrise/sunset times.<br />
9. Tap on the left top hand corner on the main window or scroll to the right to view <strong>Settings</strong>, Sign In/Sign Up, location, and other options.</p>
<h2><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_to_Use_Yahoo_Weather_App_on_iOS"></span>How to Use&#xA0;Yahoo Weather App on iOS<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">1. Go to&#xA0;App Store, or use the iTunes program on your Mac to install the app on iPhone and iOS.<br />
Use this link instead: <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/yahoo-weather/id628677149?mt=8" target="_blank" rel="noopener">https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/yahoo-weather/id628677149?mt=8</a>.<br />
2. <strong>Accept Permissions</strong> to allow the Weather app to see your location to provide local weather information.<br />
3. Choose whether or not you prefer <strong>weather notifications</strong> to appear on your phone by selecting <strong>Enable</strong> or <strong>Disable</strong>.<br />
4. Monitor weather from all over the world by selecting pre-chosen cities. You can select any other city by pressing on the <strong>plus sign in the top-right corner</strong> of the screen on the main window.<br />
5. Check out the main window to see the temperature on the bottom left of the screen. <strong>Sliding to the right</strong> provides <strong>Settings options</strong>, such as changing the temperature reading from Fahrenheit to Celsius.<br />
6. <strong>Scroll to the left</strong> to switch between your chosen city locations.<br />
7. <strong>Sliding to the bottom</strong> provides more detailed weather information for each city, such as: sun and moon information, wind pressure and speeds, along with a UV Index and local map.</p><!-- Ezoic - wp_under_second_paragraph - under_second_paragraph --><div id="ezoic-pub-ad-placeholder-111" data-inserter-version="2" data-placement-location="under_second_paragraph"></div><script data-ezoic="1">ezstandalone.cmd.push(function () { ezstandalone.showAds(111); });</script><!-- End Ezoic - wp_under_second_paragraph - under_second_paragraph -->
