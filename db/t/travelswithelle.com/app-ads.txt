<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">Looking to travel more with less? How about slowing down and experiencing more in your travels?</h3>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center has-text-color" id="block-fbcbf1a4-00c4-44e6-b228-134ab2a5aecf" style="color:#b78b09"><strong><span style="background-color:rgba(0, 0, 0, 0)" class="has-inline-color has-white-color">I&#8217;m glad you&#8217;re here.</span></strong></h3>



<p><strong>Hi, I’m Elle.</strong> I love exploring this beautiful thing called Planet Earth. </p>



<p>My goal is to help busy people like you plan affordable yet totally memorable adventures filled with epic views, incredible food, and unique cultural experiences that go way beyond the usual tourist stops.</p>



<p><strong>Travels With Elle</strong> is a travel blog that provides helpful travel guides with an off-the-beaten-path focus. I write about all things <strong>California, West Coast, Mexico, Asia&#8211;yes, the entire continent is my second home&#8211;and beyond</strong>. </p>



<p>Whether it’s uncovering hidden gems in a popular city or sharing lesser-known road trip stops, my guides focus on experiences you won’t find on average attraction lists. </p>



<p>I believe travel is about more than just checking boxes—it’s about discovering what makes a destination truly special, away from the crowds. On my blog, you’ll find practical travel tips, road trip hacks, and in-depth itineraries to help you create meaningful journeys that fit your schedule and budget.</p>
