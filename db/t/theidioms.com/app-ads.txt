itemtype="https://schema.org/Article"><h1 id="title" class="page-heading">Idioms</h1><p>An idiom is a group of words, a saying, or a phrase with a symbolic rather than literal meaning that is widely recognized and used in everyday language. It is a form of artistic expression characteristic of a movement, period, individual, medium, or instrument.</p><p data-pm-slice="1 1 []">An idiom&#8217;s figurative meaning is different from the literal definition of its individual words. They convey concepts that are difficult to grasp solely through word reading. For example, the phrase &#8220;<a title="when pigs fly" href="https://www.theidioms.com/when-pigs-fly/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>when pigs fly</strong></a>&#8221; means something will never happen, a meaning that isn&#8217;t obvious without context or explanation.</p><p>Many different idioms exist, and people use them frequently in all languages. Translating them into other languages can be challenging because some meanings may be lost. Still, equivalents that fill in the gaps between languages exist. The English language has an unlimited number of idiomatic expressions.</p><p>Idiomatic expressions are the building blocks of civilization and language. They help language grow and evolve. The richness of such phrases makes communication dynamic and engaging. These colorful expressions add vivid imagery to everyday speech and offer fascinating insights into how words are used and what the speaker is thinking. There is a sense of fun and mystery about them.</p><p data-pm-slice="1 1 []"><strong>Why are idiomatic expressions challenging?</strong></p><p>They can be hard to understand because their meanings aren’t always clear <a title="right away" href="https://www.theidioms.com/right-away/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">right away</a>. This is especially true for people learning English, since these expressions often have hidden or symbolic meanings. That’s why many learners find these kinds of phrases strange and confusing at first.</p><h2 class="new">Top 10 Everyday Expressions</h2><div class="idiom"><p>This is a list of ten unique English idioms and phrases used in America.
