<p class="has-text-align-left">Paroli works as the opposite of Martingale. Instead of increasing after losses, you raise your stake after each win to maximize hot streaks. Most players reset to the original bet after two or three consecutive wins to secure profits and avoid overexposure.</p>

<div class="gb-grid-column gb-grid-column-47afa720"><div class="gb-container gb-container-47afa720">

<p class="gb-headline gb-headline-5fe071d4 gb-headline-text">D’Alembert Strategy</p>

<p class="has-text-align-left">A safer alternative to Martingale. You increase your bet by one unit after a loss and decrease it by one unit after a win (e.g., from ₹20 to ₹30, then back to ₹20). This smoothes out variance.</p>
