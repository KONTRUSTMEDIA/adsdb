Apple Taquitos</h2><h3><span id="Quien_puede_resistirse_a_un_postre_que_combina_la_suavidad_de_la_manzana_el_aroma_de_la_canela_y_el_crujiente_de_una_tortilla_doradita">Who can resist a dessert that combines the softness of apple, the aroma of cinnamon and the crunch of a golden tortilla?</span></h3><div class="adsb30" style=" margin:12px; text-align:center">
<ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8818833347061898" data-ad-slot="8244645908" data-ad-format="auto"></ins> <script type="litespeed/javascript">(adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({})</script></div><p>&#127822;&#10024; If you have ever wanted to enjoy an apple pie without complicating yourself with dough and long baking, these air fryer apple cubes are your salvation. Quick, easy and delicious, they are perfect for a sweet treat, an afternoon coffee or to surprise the little ones in the house.</p><p>In less than 20 minutes you will have an irresistible mini dessert, with a hot, juicy filling and a golden, crispy topping. Personally, the first time I made them, my family didn&rsquo;t leave even a crumb on the plate &#128523;. In addition, they are ideal because you can prepare several batches and have desserts ready at any time.</p><div id="attachment_46696" style="width: 1546px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-46696" class="size-full wp-image-46696" src="https://thermomagazine.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/seis-flautas-estilo-burrito-enrrollado-de-manz.jpg" alt="Air Fryer Apple Pie Apple Taquitos" width="1536" height="1024"><p id="caption-attachment-46696" class="wp-caption-text">Air Fryer Apple Pie Apple Taquitos</p></div><hr><h2>Why this recipe is perfect</h2><ul><li>&#9989; <strong>Quick and practical:</strong> without the need for a long oven or preparing complicated doughs.</li><li>&#9989; <strong>Irresistible texture:</strong> Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside thanks to the tortilla and the apple filling.</li><li>&#9989; <strong>Ideal for any occasion:</strong> snack, express dessert or to accompany a coffee.</li><li>&#9989; <strong>Flexible:</strong> You can add toppings such as caramel, ice cream or whipped cream.</li><li>&#127873; <strong>Bonuses:</strong> The air fryer reduces fat and speeds up cooking without losing flavor or texture.</li></ul><hr><h2>Ingredients explained</h2><ul><li>&#127823; 1 can of apple pie filling (595 g approx.)</li><li>&#127790; 6 soft flour tortillas, medium size</li><li>&#129480; 4 tablespoons melted butter</li><li>&#127852; &frac12; cup sugar</li><li>&#129348; 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon</li></ul><p><em>Grades:</em> You can use whole wheat tortillas for a healthier version or add chopped nuts to the filling for a crunchy touch.</p><hr><h2>Equipment and preparation</h2><p><strong>What you need:</strong></p><div class="adsb30" style=" margin:12px; text-align:center">
<ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8818833347061898" data-ad-slot="8244645908" data-ad-format="auto"></ins> <script type="litespeed/javascript">(adsbygoogle=window.adsbygoogle||[]).push({})</script></div><ul><li>Airfryer with enough basket to place the taquitos without piling up.</li><li>Kitchen brush to spread butter.</li><li>Flat plate for mixing sugar and cinnamon.</li></ul><p><strong>Previous preparation:</strong> Spread the tortillas and measure the amount of filling so that the taquitos are uniform. Mix sugar and cinnamon in advance to speed up the batter.</p><hr><h2>Step by step instructions</h2><div style="background-color: #87ceeb; color: black; padding: 10px; margin: 10px 0px; border-radius: 8px;"><strong>&#9312; Prepare the tortillas</strong><p>Place the tortillas flat on a surface and place 2 tablespoons of apple filling in the center of each one. Don&rsquo;t overload so you can roll them well.</p></div><div style="background-color: #87ceeb; color: black; padding: 10px; margin: 10px 0px; border-radius: 8px;"><strong>&#9313; Roll up</strong><p>Roll the tortillas tightly like a taquito or small burrito. Press lightly to seal them and prevent the filling from opening when cooking.</p></div><div style="background-color: #87ceeb; color: black; padding: 10px; margin: 10px 0px; border-radius: 8px;"><strong>&#9314; Spread and coat</strong><p>Brush each cube with melted butter. Mix sugar and cinnamon on a flat plate and spread the cubes, coating them well on all sides.</p></div><div style="background-color: #87ceeb; color: black; padding: 10px; margin: 10px 0px; border-radius: 8px;"><strong>&#9315; Cooking in the air fryer</strong><p>Preheat the air fryer to 175&deg;C. Place the cubes seam side down and cook for 9&ndash;11 minutes until golden brown. Flip them over and cook 3&ndash;4 more minutes to achieve the desired crispiness.</p></div><div style="background-color: #87ceeb; color: black; padding: 10px; margin: 10px 0px; border-radius: 8px;"><strong>&#9316; Serve</strong><p>Serve hot. Optionally, drizzle with caramel, cinnamon maple syrup, whipped cream, or vanilla ice cream. Enjoy a mini apple pie in less than 20 minutes &#128523;.</p></div><hr><h2>Pro tips and troubleshooting</h2><p>&#127775; <strong>Secrets for success:</strong></p><ul><li>Use fresh, soft tortillas, they roll better and don&rsquo;t break.</li><li>Don&rsquo;t overload with filling; This way you prevent it from escaping when cooking.</li><li>Brush butter evenly for perfect browning and extra flavor.</li></ul><p>&#10060; <strong>Common mistakes:</strong></p><ul><li><strong>Problem:</strong> open taquito<br>
<strong>Cause:</strong> overfill<br>
<strong>Solution:</strong> use less quantity and seal the edges well</li><li><strong>Problem:</strong> it&rsquo;s not crispy<br>
<strong>Cause:</strong> little oil or low temperature<br>
<strong>Solution:</strong> brush with butter and set temperature to 175&ndash;180 &deg;C</li></ul><hr><h2>Variations and customizations</h2><p>&#128293; <strong>Extra caramel version:</strong> Add pieces of caramel into the filling before rolling.</p><p>&#127793; <strong>Healthy version:</strong> Use whole wheat tortillas and apple filling with no added sugar.</p><p>&#129473; <strong>Kids-friendly:</strong> Top with powdered sugar instead of cinnamon for a sweeter touch.</p><p>&#129381; <strong>Tropical version:</strong> Add grated coconut to the cinnamon sugar mixture before coating.</p><p>&#129383; <strong>Meal prep:</strong> prepare the taquitos and freeze them raw, cook directly from frozen in air fryer, increasing 2&ndash;3 minutes.</p><hr><h2>Serve and accompaniments</h2><p>Present the hot taquitos on a pretty plate, optionally sprinkled with icing sugar or extra cinnamon.
