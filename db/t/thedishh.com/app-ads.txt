	<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/02/19/dominion-ascension-author-interview/" rel="bookmark">Relentless Vision: Inside Dominion: Ascension and the Future of AI Power</a></h2>		</div><!-- .entry-header-wrap -->
		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/june/">June</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-02-19T22:20:35+00:00">February 19, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->
	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span data-contrast="auto">D.A. Murray is an emerging voice in feminist speculative fiction who masterfully balances her roles as a tech executive, debut novelist, and influential digital content creator. </span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Her debut novel, “Dominion: Ascension,” introduces a bold new narrative that reimagines gender dynamics in a post-apocalyptic world, challenging traditional power structures and exploring themes of female leadership and resilience. </span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Murray’s work resonates with readers seeking stories that champion feminine strength, autonomy, and societal transformation.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW107823635 BCX8" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW107823635 BCX8">Part 1 – You and your book</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW107823635 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW132518216 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW132518216 BCX8">Q. One word that best describes you:</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW132518216 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW4070340 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW4070340 BCX8">Relentless.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW4070340 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW246734913 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW246734913 BCX8">Q.  What makes your book a must-read? </span></span><span class="EOP SCXW246734913 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8">Dominion: Ascension holds a mirror to where technology, power, and gender politics are quietly headed. </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8">It’s</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8"> not just speculative fiction—it feels uncomfortably plausible. Readers </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8">don’t</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW8831564 BCX8"> just follow a story; they wrestle with the ethical future of humanity.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW8831564 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW153387208 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW153387208 BCX8">Q. What is your advice for balancing high-demand careers and creativity?</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW153387208 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW97490353 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW97490353 BCX8">Stop waiting for “free time.” Creativity survives in stolen moments. I wrote between meetings, during flights, late at night, and early mornings. Discipline, not inspiration, is what allows creativity to coexist with a demanding career.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW97490353 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW189730293 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW189730293 BCX8">Q. Any ritual, like a specific scented candle, preferred writing place, or drink that you kept through writing? </span></span><span class="EOP SCXW189730293 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW99522269 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW99522269 BCX8">A quiet room, low light, and tea. I wrote most of *Dominion* at </span><span class="NormalTextRun ContextualSpellingAndGrammarErrorV2Themed SCXW99522269 BCX8">night  and</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW99522269 BCX8"> in the early mornings when the world was still. The silence helped me hear the characters clearly.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW99522269 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong>Q. <span class="TextRun SCXW198589644 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW198589644 BCX8"> </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW198589644 BCX8">You’ve</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW198589644 BCX8"> called AI “a Frankenstein.” Why?</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW198589644 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW21921702 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW21921702 BCX8">Because we are building something incredibly powerful without fully understanding the ethical, social, and human cost. Like Frankenstein’s creation, AI is born from brilliance but carries the risk of outgrowing our control and our moral preparedness.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW21921702 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong>Q. <span class="TextRun SCXW21179376 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW21179376 BCX8">Why did you create a world where the most revered male role is reproductive? </span></span><span class="EOP SCXW21179376 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8">To flip power </span><span class="NormalTextRun ContextualSpellingAndGrammarErrorV2Themed SCXW224196924 BCX8">structures</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8"> </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8">we’ve</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8"> accepted for centuries. When you reverse who is valued for reproduction, readers are forced to confront how deeply society </span><span class="NormalTextRun ContextualSpellingAndGrammarErrorV2Themed SCXW224196924 BCX8">ties</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW224196924 BCX8"> worth to biology, control, and utility.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW224196924 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong>Q. <span class="TextRun SCXW152186729 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW152186729 BCX8">If there is a movie adaptation of your book, who do you think would be perfect for the lead roles?</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW152186729 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW93787278 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW93787278 BCX8">I envision a leading and supporting cast with emotional depth —actors like Zendaya, Keke Palmer, Tessa Thompson, John Boyega, Michael B. </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW93787278 BCX8">Jordan</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW93787278 BCX8"> or Aaron Pierre who can portray intelligence, quiet resistance, and moral conflict without needing to say much.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW93787278 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>
<p><strong>Q. <span class="NormalTextRun SCXW240942394 BCX8">You’ve</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW240942394 BCX8"> said Dominion is built “on the back of technology.” What do you mean?</span></strong></p>
<p><span class="TextRun SCXW15428484 BCX8" lang="EN-PH" xml:lang="EN-PH" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW15428484 BCX8">Every system of control in Dominion is enabled by technology—data tracking, reproductive science, surveillance, and AI governance. </span><span class="NormalTextRun ContextualSpellingAndGrammarErrorV2Themed SCXW15428484 BCX8">The dystopia</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW15428484 BCX8"> </span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW15428484 BCX8">doesn’t</span><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW15428484 BCX8"> happen despite technology; it happens because of it.</span></span><span class="EOP SCXW15428484 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;201341983&#34;:0,&#34;335559739&#34;:0,&#34;335559740&#34;:240}"> </span></p>

		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/headlines/">headlines</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-01-16T10:59:01+00:00">January 16, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The global chemicals industry continues to expand, driven by rising industrial demand and the growing need for versatile chemical intermediates. Among these, acetic acid stands out as a key commodity chemical widely used across industries such as textiles, food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and plastics. In 2023, the industry supporting acetic acid was valued at USD 16.36 billion and is projected to reach USD 28.89 billion by 2032, growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.52% over the forecast period from 2024 to 2032. This robust growth reflects the increasing importance of acetic acid as a fundamental building block in manufacturing and industrial applications.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The</span><a href="https://www.snsinsider.com/reports/acetic-acid-market-5975"> <b>Acetic Acid Market</b></a><span style="font-weight: 400"> plays a critical role in the production of a wide variety of derivatives, including vinyl acetate monomer (VAM), acetic anhydride, and ester compounds, which serve as precursors for adhesives, coatings, textiles, and packaging materials. Its versatility and relatively low production cost make it an indispensable chemical in modern manufacturing. Additionally, acetic acid is widely used in the food and beverage sector as a preservative, flavoring agent, and pH regulator, further reinforcing its importance across both industrial and consumer applications.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Several factors are driving the growth of the acetic acid industry. One major driver is the increasing demand for vinyl acetate monomer, which is primarily used in adhesives, paints, coatings, and textiles. As construction, automotive, and consumer goods sectors continue to expand globally, the demand for VAM and other acetic acid derivatives is expected to rise. Furthermore, the growing food processing industry is contributing to increased consumption of acetic acid, especially in regions with rising population and urbanization trends. It is commonly used in pickling, preserving, and as an acidity regulator, highlighting its critical role in both industrial and household applications.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Sustainability initiatives and advances in production technology are also shaping industry growth. Traditional acetic acid production relies heavily on methanol carbonylation, but newer bio-based production methods using renewable feedstocks are gaining traction. These methods not only reduce carbon emissions but also align with global sustainability goals, offering manufacturers an opportunity to cater to eco-conscious consumers and industrial buyers. As the chemical industry places greater emphasis on green manufacturing practices, bio-based acetic acid is expected to play an increasingly prominent role.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Regionally, Asia-Pacific dominates the acetic acid industry due to rapid industrialization, urbanization, and growing manufacturing and construction activities. Countries such as China and India are major producers and consumers of acetic acid, supporting a significant portion of global demand. North America and Europe, while more mature <a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/08/geocomposites-strengthening-modern-infrastructure-with-advanced-engineering-solutions/">markets</a>, continue to maintain steady demand due to the widespread use of acetic acid derivatives in adhesives, paints, pharmaceuticals, and food processing. The rising focus on sustainable production technologies and government regulations in these regions further encourages the adoption of eco-friendly and high-efficiency manufacturing methods.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Despite the strong growth prospects, the acetic acid industry faces certain challenges. The reliance on fossil fuel-derived feedstocks and fluctuations in raw material prices can affect production costs and profitability. Additionally, environmental regulations regarding emissions, waste management, and chemical handling are becoming more stringent, prompting manufacturers to invest in cleaner, more efficient production technologies. However, these challenges also create opportunities for innovation, particularly in the development of bio-based and renewable acetic acid production processes that are both cost-effective and environmentally friendly.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Looking ahead, the acetic acid industry is expected to continue its growth trajectory, driven by rising industrial demand, technological advancements, and sustainability initiatives. The projected increase from USD 16.36 billion in 2023 to USD 28.89 billion by 2032 underscores its critical role in manufacturing, food processing, and chemical production. As industries increasingly focus on durable materials, eco-friendly production methods, and versatile chemical intermediates, acetic acid is poised to remain a foundational component of industrial and consumer products worldwide.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">In conclusion, the acetic acid industry represents a vital intersection of industrial chemistry, sustainability, and consumer applications. With a CAGR of 6.52% from 2024 to 2032, the sector is set for significant expansion, driven by industrial demand, innovations in production technology, and the growing adoption of bio-based processes. As a versatile chemical used across multiple industries, acetic acid will continue to play a crucial role in manufacturing, food and beverage applications, and sustainable industrial practices, reinforcing its position as a cornerstone of the global chemical landscape.</span></p>

	<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/15/unveiling-alan-hovhaness-a-composers-life-of-music-love-and-legacy/" rel="bookmark">Unveiling Alan Hovhaness: A Composer’s Life of Music, Love, and Legacy</a></h2>		</div><!-- .entry-header-wrap -->
		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/june/">June</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-01-15T19:00:33+00:00">January 15, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->
	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><strong><span class="TextRun SCXW168052296 BCX8" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW168052296 BCX8">Q. What makes Alan Hovhaness: Unveiling One of the Great Composers of the 20th Century a must-read? </span></span><span class="EOP SCXW168052296 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Alan Hovhaness would tell Hinako Hovhaness personal stories about other famous composers. Many of the stories were not complimentary stories found in books but rather passed down by word of mouth. Hinako believed that Alan’s personal first -hand stories needed to also be told. Many knew of his compositions, but not many knew his personality and interests. This book was an opportunity to provide an insight into Alan Hovhaness’ legacy. </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The book started out to be more of a documentary, however due to the subject matter it turned into a heartfelt love story. The book transitions through the different forms of love we experience: romantic, family, elderly caring and longing. Hinako uses a deeply passionate style of writing to capture the reader into the emotion of the story.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The book also tells a story of a classical composer that was bitterly ridiculed during a time when his music was not in fashion. Rather than submit to social acceptance he stuck to his beliefs and followed the path of beautiful and healing music. This led him to travel to India, Japan and Korea to study the ancient music styles. Through absorbing these cultures and being exposed to their beliefs, Hovhaness was subjected to many sensational experiences and adventures.  </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><strong>Q. <span class="TextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8">If you could give this book to one world leader, who would it be and why?</span></span><span class="TextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8"> </span></span><span class="TextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8" lang="EN-US" xml:lang="EN-US" data-contrast="auto"><span class="NormalTextRun SCXW61047260 BCX8">  </span></span><span class="EOP SCXW61047260 BCX8" data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></strong></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The question does not specify dead or alive, or world leader in what, therefore I am selecting Leonardo DiVinci. Of course, Alan was Atalante Migliorotti in a previous carnation, who was a musician and pupil of Leonardo. Atalante sang the lead role in the opera on the subject of Orpheus in 1491 (Hovhaness’s Opus 155 </span><i><span data-contrast="auto">Meditation on Orpheus</span></i><span data-contrast="auto">). I believe Leonardo would be very happy to read about one of his students.</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. What was the hardest part of co-authoring the book?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">  </span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">My mother, Hinako Hovhaness wrote all the book text and had it in draft form. Due to her health issues, she could not publish the book prior to her passing. My wife, daughter and I completed the text editing, sorting through hundreds of photographs, and worked with the publisher to design the cover, dust cover and custom boxes.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The hardest part was editing the text. My mother had somewhat choppy English but wanted to retain some of her “Orientalness” in her writing. Although the book has high grades for content, we struggled to make the writing readable and retain her style. </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. What is the most enlightening/inspirational story you tell in this book?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Alan Hovhaness earned a scholarship to Tanglewood in 1942. During a class presentation of one of his compositions his classmates Aaron Copeland and Leonard Bernstein ridiculed his composition as “ghetto” music. Hovhaness was humiliated and was looked upon as an outsider. This led to his leaving Tanglewood, and he burned over 1000 of his compositions. </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">During the early 1940’s Hovhaness established a relationship with William Saroyan, in which Hovhaness wrote music to Saroyan’s writings. Saroyan believed Hovhaness was an upcoming young composer and in the same year that Hovhaness left Tanglewood, Saroyan introduced his music to Leopold Stokowski. In January 1943 Stokowski performed Hovhaness’s Symphony No. 1 </span><i><span data-contrast="auto">Exile</span></i><span data-contrast="auto"> with the Los Angeles Philharmonic Orchestra. Stokowski performed numerous Hovhaness compositions in the years to come, which greatly helped establish Hovhaness’s career.</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. One word that best describes you.</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Consistency – During my career as an Avionics Systems Engineer, there were many engineers that were more intelligent and others that were more persuasive. However, consistency provides experience, which is essential in any field.  </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. What is the core message you want to deliver to the world through your writing/books/stories?</span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Stick to what you believe in, do not bend to social influence. You learn by failing.</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. If there is a movie adaptation of</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">your book,</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">who do you think would be perfect for the lead roles?</span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">John Lennon and Yoko Ono.</span><span data-contrast="auto"> </span><span data-contrast="auto">First, both couples have somewhat similar physical looks. It doesn’t stop there… Alan was very much into religious study and was on the same frequency as the universe, which allowed him to capture the sounds from the mountains, trees, weather and interpret that into his music. I think both Alan and John were able to express their life force through their music and writing.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Both Alan and John had prior relationships before Hinako and Yoko, but somehow these two women were able to greatly influence these remarkable men’s lives. </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. What can this teach to a motivated and mission-driven population of writers?</span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Everyone has a story, fiction or non-fiction. During my career I wrote many requirement documents which are a completely different method of writing. I learned throughout the book publishing process, and this book may not be a literary masterpiece, however it provided the experience to move on to the next level. Keep writing, learning and expand your experience.</span><span data-contrast="auto"> </span><span data-contrast="auto">  </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Movie or TV Series? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Movie – Too many personal projects, one-and-done works. </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Long walk or long car ride? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Depends on location of the walk… City – no, Country – yes. </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Comedy or Drama? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Need more Comedy. Drama cup is full. </span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. The city or the country? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Country – Peace rather than protest.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Baking or Cooking? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Cooking – I like Baking, but Cooking seems to have more possibilities.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Reading a book or watching a movie? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  I usually read non-fiction books and movies are usually fiction. It depends on my mood.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Holding a Puppy or Holding a Baby? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Puppy</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>

<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Sparkling Skin – I’m not one who is addicted to attention, but Invisibility Cloak seems somewhat devious.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Coffee or tea? </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">A.  Generally, Coffee, but am I at Starbucks or McDonalds?</span><span data-ccp-props="{&#34;335559685&#34;:720}"> </span></p>

		<p>Step into a delightfully whimsical conversation with Steven Joseph, author of <em data-start="149" data-end="168">Snoodles in Space</em>, and illustrator Andy Case. In this lighthearted and candid Q&amp;A, the creative duo shares the quirky inspirations, personal stories, and behind-the-scenes moments that shaped their laugh-out-loud children’s book. From smoked sable to transforming chocoloompadoodles, Steven and Andy reflect on humor, creativity, and the joy of storytelling that sparks imagination for readers of all ages. Steven, a lawyer by day and humorist by nature, draws on real-life moments—often inspired by his partner Carolyn’s hilarious antics—while Andy’s brilliantly detailed illustrations add layers of visual comedy that make each read a new adventure.</p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Steven:</span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. If you could give your book to one person in the entire world to read, who would it be and why?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b></p>
<p>Mel Brooks.  I think he would appreciate the humor and he is 99.  I say the age group did this book is 6-99so he just makes the cut!</p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. What makes your book unputdownable?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Andy Case’s brilliant illustrations, of course. There are so many details you can miss in the first one to two hundred reads!</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q . Who/What is your biggest inspiration in your writing?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">My partner Carolyn.  She does funny stuff all the time!</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">What would you name your boat if you had one?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The Snoodle McDoodle!</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q . What is the best/worst purchase you’ve ever made?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">The best purchase is a pound of smoked sable.  I put it on a bagel with purple onion, tomato and cream cheese and it is delicious. The worst purchase I’ve ever made hmmm….I can’t think of any that would be the worst!</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Andy:</span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q . Tell us something about you that no one else knows</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">I used to be a cocktailbarman and i can throw a bottle over my head, have it spin twice in the air and land it on the back of my hand.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. Which fictional character would be most exciting to meet in real life?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">Miss Marple or Hercule Poirot. We could solve murders together.</span></p>

<p><span data-contrast="auto">Awful.I’mrenowned for being terrible at accents. Even my English accent is terrible</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">What would be a good theme song for your life?</span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">‘don’t worry, be happy’ – bobby McFerrin</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>

<p><span data-contrast="auto">My mate once phoned me and told me to hold a can of deodorant on the back of my hand and keep spraying. I had a third degree burn after. I learned a lot about human psychology that day!</span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Rapid Fire</span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Steven:</span></b><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q. </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">One thing you can’t live without?</span></b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto"> Exercise </span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>
<p><b><span data-contrast="auto">Q.  </span></b><b><span data-contrast="auto">If you weren’t an author, what would you be?</span></b><span data-contrast="auto"> </span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">I am a lawyer so that would have to be my answer.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span></p>

		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/headlines/">headlines</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-01-08T17:07:18+00:00">January 8, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Rapid urbanization, expanding infrastructure projects, and the growing need for sustainable construction materials are driving the adoption of advanced geosynthetic solutions worldwide. Among these, geocomposites have emerged as a critical material in modern civil engineering due to their multifunctional properties and cost-effectiveness. The Geocomposites Market size was valued at USD 445.3 million in 2023 and is expected to reach USD 776.0 million by 2032, growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.4% during the forecast period from 2024 to 2032. This steady growth highlights the increasing importance of geocomposites in infrastructure development and environmental protection projects.</span></p>
<p><b>Understanding Geocomposites and Their Role</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The</span><a href="https://www.snsinsider.com/reports/geocomposites-market-3057"> <b>Geocomposites Market</b></a><span style="font-weight: 400"> focuses on engineered materials formed by combining two or more geosynthetic components—such as geotextiles, geomembranes, geogrids, or geonets—into a single product. This multifunctional design allows geocomposites to perform several roles simultaneously, including drainage, filtration, separation, reinforcement, and protection. Their ability to deliver enhanced performance compared to single-component geosynthetics makes products in the Geocomposites Market highly suitable for complex and demanding construction environments.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">These materials are widely used in roads, railways, landfills, retaining walls, embankments, tunnels, and hydraulic structures. By improving soil stability and controlling water movement, geocomposites help extend the lifespan of infrastructure while reducing maintenance costs.</span></p>
<p><b>Key Drivers Supporting Growth</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">One of the primary factors driving the<a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/02/how-cloud-disaster-recovery-is-transforming-business-continuity-practices/"> growth</a> of geocomposites is the surge in global infrastructure development. Governments across both developed and emerging economies are investing heavily in transportation networks, smart cities, and public utilities. Geocomposites offer a durable and efficient solution for managing soil and water challenges commonly encountered in large-scale construction projects.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Environmental regulations are also contributing to increased adoption. Strict guidelines related to soil erosion, waste containment, and water management have encouraged engineers and contractors to use materials that meet sustainability and performance standards. Geocomposites play a vital role in landfill liners, leachate collection systems, and erosion control measures, supporting compliance with environmental norms.</span></p>
<p><b>Applications Across Construction and Environmental Sectors</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The transportation sector represents one of the largest application areas for geocomposites. In road and railway construction, they are used for drainage layers, subgrade reinforcement, and separation of soil layers. These functions help prevent water accumulation, reduce structural deformation, and improve load-bearing capacity.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">In environmental engineering, geocomposites are extensively used in landfill construction and wastewater management systems. Their high permeability and filtration efficiency enable effective drainage while preventing soil contamination. Similarly, in hydraulic applications such as canals, dams, and reservoirs, geocomposites provide protection against erosion and seepage.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Slope stabilization and retaining wall construction further highlight the versatility of geocomposites. By reinforcing soil and enhancing drainage, they help mitigate landslide risks and maintain structural integrity in challenging terrains.</span></p>
<p><b>Regional Insights and Market Trends</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">North America and Europe currently hold significant shares of the geocomposites market due to well-established infrastructure, stringent environmental regulations, and widespread adoption of geosynthetic technologies. These regions benefit from advanced engineering practices and high awareness regarding the long-term cost savings offered by geocomposites.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The Asia-Pacific region is expected to witness the fastest growth over the forecast period. Rapid urban expansion, increasing population, and large-scale infrastructure initiatives in countries such as China, India, and Southeast Asian nations are driving demand. Additionally, rising investments in flood control, transportation corridors, and waste management projects are accelerating adoption.</span></p>
<p><b>Challenges and Technological Advancements</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Despite strong growth prospects, the geocomposites industry faces challenges related to raw material price volatility and limited awareness in certain developing regions. In some markets, traditional construction materials are still preferred due to lower upfront costs, even though geocomposites offer superior long-term performance.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">However, ongoing technological advancements are addressing these challenges. Manufacturers are focusing on product innovation to improve durability, strength, and ease of installation. The development of customized geocomposites tailored to specific project requirements is further enhancing their value proposition.</span></p>
<p><b>Future Outlook</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Looking ahead, the geocomposites market is poised for sustained expansion through 2032. The increasing emphasis on resilient infrastructure, climate-adaptive construction practices, and sustainable development will continue to drive demand. As engineers and policymakers recognize the long-term benefits of geocomposites, their adoption is expected to move beyond large-scale projects into more routine construction applications.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">With the market projected to grow from USD 445.3 million in 2023 to USD 776.0 million by 2032 at a CAGR of 6.4%, geocomposites are set to play a vital role in shaping the future of global infrastructure. Their ability to combine performance, durability, and environmental responsibility positions them as an indispensable material in modern civil and environmental engineering.</span></p>

	<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/03/early-signs-of-substance-use-concerns-you-should-not-ignore/" rel="bookmark">Early Signs of Substance Use Concerns You Should Not Ignore</a></h2>		</div><!-- .entry-header-wrap -->
		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/digitaleditor/">Digital Editor</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-01-03T18:41:03+00:00">January 3, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->
	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Substance use concerns rarely appear overnight. For many people, harmful patterns develop slowly and quietly, often disguised as stress relief, social habits, or ways to cope with emotional pain. You may tell yourself everything is under control, especially if life still looks functional on the outside. Yet early warning signs often show up long before addiction fully takes hold.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Recognizing these signs early can make a meaningful difference. Early support gives you more options, more flexibility, and a greater chance to change course without reaching a crisis point. Understanding what to look for helps you respond with awareness rather than fear or denial.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">Why Early Signs Are Easy to Miss</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Substance use often starts for understandable reasons. You may be trying to relax, manage anxiety, cope with grief, or feel more comfortable socially. Because these motivations feel reasonable, early warning signs can be easy to overlook.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You may also compare yourself to stereotypes about addiction and conclude that your situation does not qualify. You might think addiction only applies when someone loses a job, gets arrested, or experiences severe consequences. In reality, most people who develop substance use disorders once lived in the early gray area where things felt manageable but slowly became harder to control.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Paying attention early is not about labeling yourself. It is about noticing patterns and being honest about how substance use is affecting your life.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400">Subtle Changes in Behavior</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">One of the earliest signs of a substance use concern is a shift in how often or how intentionally you use. You may notice that substances are becoming more central to your routine.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Some signs include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Using more frequently than you planned</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Needing substances to relax, sleep, or feel normal</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Looking forward to using as a reward or relief</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling uneasy or irritable when you cannot use</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Structuring social plans around access to substances</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">These changes often happen gradually. Each step may seem small on its own, but together they signal increasing reliance.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400">Emotional and Mental Health Shifts</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Substance use and emotional health are closely connected. Early substance use concerns often show up through changes in mood, emotional regulation, or stress tolerance.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You may notice:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Increased anxiety or irritability</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Mood swings or emotional numbness</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Difficulty coping with stress without substances</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Using substances to avoid feelings or thoughts</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling guilty or ashamed after using</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">These emotional shifts can create a cycle where substances temporarily relieve discomfort but increase emotional distress over time.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400">Changes in Relationships</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Relationships often reflect early substance use concerns before you notice them yourself. You may find yourself becoming more secretive or defensive when others express concern.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Warning signs can include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Hiding or minimizing your use</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling annoyed when loved ones ask questions</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Withdrawing from people who do not use</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Spending less time on meaningful connections</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Increased conflict related to substance use</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Even subtle relational tension can be a sign that something important needs attention.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400">Increased Tolerance and Loss of Control</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Tolerance develops when your body adapts to a substance and requires more to achieve the same effect. This can happen quietly and quickly.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You may notice:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Needing more to feel relaxed or satisfied</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Using longer than intended</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Breaking personal rules around use</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling surprised by how much you consumed</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Loss of control does not always mean daily use. It often begins with small moments where your intentions and actions no longer align.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400">Impact on Daily Functioning</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Early substance use concerns often affect daily life in ways that are easy to rationalize. You may still meet responsibilities, but things feel harder than they used to.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Possible signs include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Decreased motivation or focus</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Trouble sleeping or irregular routines</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Neglecting hobbies or self care</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Using substances to get through the day</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling drained or foggy more often</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">When substances begin to interfere with your energy, clarity, or sense of purpose, it is worth paying attention.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">Internal Warning Signs You Might Ignore</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Some of the most important early signs happen internally. These may never be visible to others, but they matter deeply.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You may notice:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Thinking about cutting back but not following through</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling uneasy about your use but avoiding the topic</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Comparing yourself to others to justify behavior</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Promising yourself it will not happen again</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Feeling relief when substances are available</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">These internal signals often reflect growing ambivalence and concern, even if you have not voiced it out loud.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">Why Early Support Matters</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Addressing substance use concerns early can prevent escalation and reduce harm. Early counseling focuses on awareness, emotional regulation, coping skills, and understanding the role substances play in your life.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You do not need to wait until things fall apart to seek help. In fact, early support often leads to better outcomes and less disruption.</span></p>
<p><a href="https://www.recoveryhill.info/substance-abuse/"><span style="font-weight: 400">Substance abuse counseling</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400"> clients often emphasizes prevention and early intervention. Counseling helps you explore patterns without judgment and identify healthier ways to meet emotional needs.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">What Substance Abuse Counseling Offers Early On</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Early counseling looks different from crisis driven treatment. It is collaborative, flexible, and focused on insight and growth rather than labels.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">In counseling, you can expect to:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Explore your relationship with substances</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Identify emotional and situational triggers</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Learn coping tools for stress and cravings</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Strengthen emotional regulation skills</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Address underlying anxiety, <a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/02/trauma-therapy-explained-what-healing-can-look-like/">trauma</a>, or depression</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><span style="font-weight: 400">Set realistic and personalized goals</span><span style="font-weight: 400"><br/>
</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Counseling creates space to reflect honestly without pressure or fear. You remain in control of the process.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">Overcoming Shame and Fear</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Many people avoid seeking help because of shame. You may worry about being judged or labeled. In reality, counseling is a supportive space designed to meet you where you are.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Substance use concerns exist on a spectrum. Seeking help early is a sign of self awareness and strength, not failure. Counseling helps reduce shame by normalizing the human need for coping and support.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400">Listening to the Signs</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Early warning signs are invitations to pause and reflect. They are not verdicts about who you are or where your life is headed. Paying attention now gives you the chance to make changes before patterns deepen.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">If you notice yourself resonating with these signs, you are not alone. Many people experience this stage, and many find that early counseling helps them regain clarity, balance, and confidence.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">You deserve support before things become overwhelming. Listening to early signs allows you to protect your wellbeing and move forward with intention.</span></p>

	<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="https://thedishh.com/2026/01/02/how-cloud-disaster-recovery-is-transforming-business-continuity-practices/" rel="bookmark">How Cloud Disaster Recovery Is Transforming Business Continuity Practices</a></h2>		</div><!-- .entry-header-wrap -->
		<div class="entry-meta">
			By <a class="url fn n" href="https://thedishh.com/author/headlines/">headlines</a> on <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2026-01-02T23:50:47+00:00">January 2, 2026</time>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->
	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><span style="font-weight: 400">In an era where digital operations underpin nearly every aspect of modern enterprises, business continuity has evolved from a back-office consideration into a boardroom priority. Organizations today face an expanding array of risks—from cyberattacks and system failures to natural disasters and geopolitical disruptions—that can halt operations in minutes. Against this backdrop, cloud disaster recovery has emerged as a transformative force, reshaping how businesses plan for, respond to, and recover from unexpected disruptions.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Traditionally, disaster recovery relied heavily on on-premise infrastructure, secondary data centers, and manual recovery procedures. These approaches were capital-intensive, complex to manage, and often limited in scalability. Recovery time objectives (RTOs) and recovery point objectives (RPOs) were constrained by physical infrastructure and geographic proximity. As digital ecosystems grew more distributed and data volumes surged, these traditional models struggled to keep pace with modern resilience expectations.</span></p>
<p><a href="https://www.snsinsider.com/reports/cloud-disaster-recovery-market-1540"><span style="font-weight: 400">Cloud disaster recovery</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400"> fundamentally changes this equation by leveraging virtualized, geographically dispersed environments. Instead of tying recovery strategies to a single physical location, organizations can replicate critical workloads and data across multiple cloud regions. This architectural shift allows enterprises to rethink continuity planning as a dynamic, always-available capability rather than a static insurance policy that is only activated during crises.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Another significant transformation lies in agility. Cloud-based recovery environments enable rapid testing and validation of disaster recovery plans without interrupting live operations. Businesses can simulate failure scenarios, validate recovery processes, and refine response strategies on an ongoing basis. This continuous testing culture improves organizational readiness and reduces the uncertainty that historically surrounded disaster recovery execution.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Security considerations have also played a pivotal role in accelerating the adoption of cloud disaster recovery. As ransomware attacks and data breaches become more sophisticated, organizations increasingly view recovery readiness as a core component of cyber resilience. The ability to restore clean data copies from isolated cloud environments provides an added layer of confidence in the face of malicious threats, reinforcing trust in digital operations.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">At the same time, the growing reliance on cloud disaster recovery reflects broader shifts in enterprise IT strategy. Hybrid and multi-cloud environments are now common, and disaster recovery strategies are evolving to align with these complex architectures. Rather than focusing solely on infrastructure protection, businesses are emphasizing application-level resilience and data integrity across interconnected platforms.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Cloud Disaster Recovery Market was valued at USD 9.7 billion in 2023 and is expected to reach USD 71.8 billion by 2032, growing at a CAGR of 24.94% from 2024-2032. This rapid expansion underscores how critical disaster recovery has become in the digital economy. The surge in market value reflects rising cloud adoption across industries, increased awareness of downtime costs, and stricter regulatory expectations around data availability and protection. Enterprises are recognizing that disruptions no longer represent rare events but inevitable challenges that require robust, scalable preparedness. As digital transformation accelerates, organizations are generating and relying on unprecedented volumes of data, making resilient recovery mechanisms essential for operational survival. The strong compound annual growth rate also highlights how disaster recovery is transitioning from a niche IT function to a strategic investment area. Financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and e-commerce sectors are particularly driving this growth as they depend heavily on real-time systems and uninterrupted access to data. Overall, market momentum signals a long-term shift in how organizations view risk, resilience, and continuity planning in an increasingly cloud-centric world.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Beyond market growth, cloud disaster recovery is influencing organizational culture. Business continuity is no longer perceived as solely an IT responsibility; it increasingly involves executive leadership, compliance teams, and operational managers. This cross-functional engagement ensures that recovery priorities align closely with business impact, customer expectations, and regulatory obligations. As a result, disaster recovery planning becomes more integrated into enterprise-wide risk management frameworks.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">The transformation is also evident in how recovery metrics are defined. Instead of focusing only on system restoration, organizations are emphasizing continuity of critical business functions and customer-facing services. This shift aligns recovery objectives more closely with real-world business outcomes, such as minimizing revenue loss, preserving brand reputation, and maintaining customer trust during disruptions.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Cloud disaster recovery will continue to shape business continuity practices as organizations navigate an increasingly unpredictable environment. Advances in automation, analytics, and orchestration are expected to further streamline recovery processes, while global cloud infrastructure expansion will enhance geographic resilience. More importantly, the mindset around disaster recovery is evolving—from reactive recovery to proactive resilience.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">In conclusion, cloud disaster recovery is not merely a technological upgrade; it represents a fundamental transformation in how organizations approach continuity and risk. By enabling scalable resilience, continuous preparedness, and business-aligned recovery strategies, it is redefining what it <a href="https://thedishh.com/2025/07/10/forget-social-media-your-financial-success-starts-here/">means to be operationally ready in the digital age</a>. As disruptions become more frequent and costly, cloud disaster recovery will remain a cornerstone of modern business continuity practices.</span></p>
