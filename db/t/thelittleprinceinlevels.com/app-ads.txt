<p style="text-align: center;">Students can read this book in <a href="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/how-to-use/">three levels</a>.</p></div>

				<div class="et_pb_text_inner"><p style="text-align: center;">Level 1</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>CHAPTER 1 – HAT</strong></p></div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_audio_0 et_pb_audio_module clearfix et_pb_bg_layout_dark et_pb_audio_no_image">
				
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_audio_module_content et_audio_container">
					<h2 class="et_pb_module_header">Chapter 1</h2>
					
					<audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-601-1" preload="none" style="width: 100%;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/LP-L1-Ch1-slow.mp3?_=1" /><a href="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/LP-L1-Ch1-slow.mp3">https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/LP-L1-Ch1-slow.mp3</a></audio>
				</div>
			</div><div class="et_pb_module et_pb_text et_pb_text_2  et_pb_text_align_left et_pb_bg_layout_light">
				
				
				
				
				<div class="et_pb_text_inner">I am six years old. I see a nice picture. The picture is in a book. It is a picture of a big snake. The snake eats an animal. Here is the picture.
<p style="text-align: center;"><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-614" src="https://thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/OBR1-300x162.jpg" alt="" width="381" height="206" srcset="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/OBR1-300x162.jpg 300w, https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/OBR1.jpg 647w" sizes="(max-width: 381px) 100vw, 381px" /></p>
The book says, “Snakes eat the <strong>whole</strong> animal. And they sleep <strong>for</strong> six months.”

I think about the life in the forest. I make my first picture. This is my picture number one.
<p style="text-align: center;"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-750" src="https://thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP3-Hat-300x96.png" alt="" width="346" height="111" srcset="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP3-Hat-300x96.png 300w, https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP3-Hat.png 376w" sizes="(max-width: 346px) 100vw, 346px" /></p>
I show my fantastic picture to one person. And I <strong>ask</strong> this person, “Are you <strong>scared</strong>?”

This person answers, “How can I be scared? It is a <strong>hat</strong>.”

My picture is not a picture of a hat. It is a picture of a big snake. An elephant is <strong>inside</strong> the snake. Then I make my picture number two. The picture shows the inside of the big snake. Now people understand. This is my picture number two.
<p style="text-align: center;"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-751" src="https://thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP4-Elephant-300x94.png" alt="" width="367" height="115" srcset="https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP4-Elephant-300x94.png 300w, https://www.thelittleprinceinlevels.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/LP4-Elephant.png 384w" sizes="(max-width: 367px) 100vw, 367px" /></p>
People tell me what they <strong>think</strong>. They tell me <strong>that</strong> it is not good to make pictures. They tell me that it is better to study geography, history, maths and grammar. After these words, I don&#8217;t want to make pictures. I don&#8217;t want to make pictures because my picture number one and picture number two are not good for the people. People don&#8217;t understand <strong>simple</strong> things.

And now, I am not a child. I am a big person. I have a job. I am a pilot. I <strong>fly</strong> planes. I fly in many countries. And it is <strong>true</strong> that geography is good for me. Geography can help me if I am <strong>lost</strong> in the night.</div>

				<div class="et_pb_text_inner">In my life, I work with people every day. The people are often <strong>serious</strong>. <strong>Sometimes</strong> I meet a person who is <strong>a little</strong> normal. When I meet this person, I show him my picture number one. I always have this picture with me. I want to know if this person understands life. But the person always says, “It is a hat.” Then I <strong>never</strong> speak to this person about big snakes or forests or <strong>stars</strong>. And we speak about work, sport and family. And the person is happy. For <strong>him</strong> I am a normal man.

I live <strong>alone</strong>. I don’t know people for an open conversation. But one day it all <strong>changes</strong>. I have an <strong>accident</strong> in the Sahara Desert.

Something is <strong>broken</strong> in my plane. I have nobody in the plane with me. It is not <strong>easy</strong> to <strong>repair</strong> the plane alone. But I must do it. It is a question of life or <strong>death</strong>. I have water for only a week.</div>
