            <p class="text-gray-700 leading-relaxed mb-3">
                The Youtube Playlist Lenght works by using simple math to figure out how long it will take to finish your audio or
                video at a certain speed. Here’s how it does that:
            </p>
            <ol class="list-decimal ml-6">

                    <p class="mt-2">We first convert the total time of your audiobook or video into seconds. The formula
                        is:
                    </p>
                    <p class="mt-2 italic">Total Length (seconds) = (Hours × 3600) + (Minutes × 60) + Seconds</p>
                </li>
                <li class="mb-4">
                    <h2 class="font-bold">Listening Time:</h2>
                    <p class="mt-2">To calculate the new time at your selected speed, we use this formula:</p>
                    <div class="mt-2 italic flex items-center">
                        <span>Listening Time (seconds) = </span>
                        <div class="flex flex-col items-center mx-2">
                            <span>Total Length (seconds)</span>
                            <hr class="border-t-2 border-black w-full my-1">
                            <span>Playback Speed</span>
                        </div>
                    </div>
                </li>
                <li class="mb-4">
                    <strong>Time Saved:</strong>
                    <p class="mt-2">If you choose a faster speed, you'll save time. The formula for time saved is:</p>
                    <p class="mt-2 italic">
                        Time Saved (seconds) = Total Length (seconds) − Listening Time (seconds)
                    </p>
                </li>
            </ol>
