	<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Welcome to <strong>Daily His Disciple</strong>. My name is Annie McGuire, and I write this blog for Christian women. I am passionate about teaching the scriptures and seeing women walk in the wisdom of God&#8217;s word. I will pass along the biblical truths that changed my life and continue to shape me as a woman pursuing Christ-likeness. I aim to contribute to your spiritual growth in an informative and inspirational way.</p>
<p>The goal of Daily His Disciple is to see you growing in wisdom and walking closely with Jesus every day, as devoted disciples should. I hope to encourage you with the truth of God&#8217;s word and equip you to study the Bible for yourself.</p>
<p>I pray that this blog for Christian women is a blessing to you and that it helps you grow spiritually as you seek the Lord!</p>
<p><strong>Colossians 3:16 KJV~ Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord. </strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
