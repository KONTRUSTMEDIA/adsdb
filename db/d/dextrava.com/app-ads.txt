<ol>
<li>Tap the “Post” icon from the homepage from the bottom of the app’s interface. This will help you to initiate a new thread.

<h2><span id="How_to_Reply_to_a_Thread">How to Reply to a Thread</span></h2>
<p>To make replies on threads, select the “Reply” icon and add your comment and post. If someone replies to your thread, their reply is linked to your original thread, and you can view replies by selecting the “Reply count” under your thread.</p>
<h2><span id="How_to_Delete_a_Thread">How to Delete a Thread</span></h2>
<p>If you decide to delete a thread, go to your profile by selecting the “Profile” icon from the bottom of the app’s interface. Find the thread on your profile page and select the “Menu” icon next to it to reveal the “Delete” option. Tap on “Delete”, and your thread is removed.</p>

<h2><span id="FAQs">FAQs</span></h2>
<h4><span id="1_Can_I_delete_someone_elses_reply_to_my_thread">1. Can I delete someone else’s reply to my thread?</span></h4>
<p>No, as the thread creator, you can only delete your own threads. Replies from other users will remain visible unless the original replier decides to delete their own reply.</p>
<h4><span id="2_How_can_I_change_the_visibility_of_my_thread_after_posting_it">2. How can I change the visibility of my thread after posting it?</span></h4>
<p>Once a thread is posted, you can still directly change its visibility settings. Simply find the thread on your profile page and select the “Menu” icon, then choose the “Who can reply” option. You can choose the “Anyone”, “Profiles you follow”, or “Mentioned only” option.</p>

<h4><span id="3_Are_there_any_restrictions_on_the_type_or_size_of_files_I_can_attach_to_a_thread">3. Are there any restrictions on the type or size of files I can attach to a thread?</span></h4>
<p>Instagram imposes certain limitations on the size and type of files you can attach to a thread. Ensure that your files adhere to the supported formats and file size restrictions imposed by the platform.</p>
<h4><span id="4_Is_Threads_available_on_all_versions_of_Instagram">4. Is Threads available on all versions of Instagram?</span></h4>
<p>Threads is a separate app introduced by Meta and is not available on any version of Instagram. You need to install the app directly from the Google Play Store for Android and Apple App Store for iOS. Note that Its availability may vary depending on your region.</p>
<h4><span id="5_Can_I_still_access_my_thread_if_I_change_my_privacy_settings">5. Can I still access my thread if I change my privacy settings?</span></h4>
<p>Yes, you will still be able to access your thread even if you change your privacy settings. However, keep in mind that any changes to your privacy settings may impact the visibility of your thread to other users. It’s important to review and adjust your privacy settings accordingly to ensure your desired level of audience engagement.</p>
