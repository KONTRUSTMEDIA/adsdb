			<h2>Some Notes</h2>

			<div id="username-generator-not-submitted"></div>

			<p>The regular <a href="random-username-generator.php">random username generator</a> lets you generate lists of usernames made up of words picked from lists of categories.</p>

			<p>However, most of the time people want a username which is personal, and that means something to them.</p>
			
			<p>With this tool, you can enter your own word, and then pick from the list of categories to generate usernames made up of your typed word and random words from the username category you select - or just select any word at random if you leave the category set to 'Random Category'.</p>
			
			<p>Once you've submitted the form, you can then choose from a load of additional options, such as choosing the starting letter, username length, the position of your word, and so on.</p>
			
			<h3>Bells and whistles</h3>
			
			<p>If you click on the <code>Show Bells and Whistles</code> option, there are a load of useful options, such as:</p>
			
			<ul>
				<li>Leetify
					<ul>
						<li>Swaps letters in your username for numbers</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Nice Font
					<ul>
						<li>Display with a nice font on a pretty background colour</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Plural 1st / 2nd Word
					<ul>
						<li>Stick an `s` on the end of the first or second component word</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Reverse
					<ul>
						<li>Reverse the words in the results so that cartnose becomes traceson</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Shuffle
					<ul>
						<li>Randomly rearrange the letters in the username</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Chunk
					<ul>
						<li>Return a random chunk of a word</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Number at Start / End
					<ul>
						<li>Add a random number at the start / end of your username</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Initial Capitals
					<ul>
						<li>Capitalise initial letter of each word</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
				<li>Filter Text
					<ul>
						<li>Include text you want your username results to include, and once you type in something, select if that should appear anywhere in the word, or at the start or end...</li>
					</ul>
				</li>
			</ul>
