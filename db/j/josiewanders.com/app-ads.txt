<p style="font-size:18px">Welcome! I&#8217;m Josie (she/her), a passionate traveller in my fifties who’s lived the typical life of work, marriage, mortgage, and kids. But my dreams are extraordinary, and travel is at the top of that list!</p>



<p>From weekend getaways to an &#8220;adult gap year&#8221; with my husband, I’m excited to share tips on how to travel like you&#8217;re living the high life, all while sticking to a budget.</p>



<p>I’ve explored over 60 countries, staying everywhere from hostels to five-star hotels, flying budget to first class, and dining from street food to Michelin-starred restaurants.</p>



<p style="font-size:18px">I love luxury but know how to balance it with more affordable choices. I didn’t start travelling on a shoestring until my late thirties, and now I enjoy that style of travel, especially when it leads to the occasional five-star stay or business-class flight. </p>



<p style="font-size:18px">As someone focused on value-for-money travel, I’m here to help other Gen Xers achieve this same balance! If I can do it, so can you!</p>
