				<h2 class="fsElementTitle">Register for Athletics</h2>
	</header>
	<div class="fsElementContent" >
			<p>Athletics registration is completed in Final Forms.</p>

<p><a class="fs_style_5" href="https://norththurston-wa.finalforms.com/" target="_blank">Get started on Final Forms</a></p>

<h4>Requirements for participation</h4>

<ol>
	<li>Parent/guardian and student consent forms are signed in Final Forms.</li>
	<li>Sports physical form signed by a registered physician and uploaded to Final Forms.&nbsp;<em>Physicals are good for 2 years</em>.</li>
	<li>All fines/fees paid online <a data-page-name="Fines, Fees, &amp; Lunches (payments)" href="/fs/pages/4104">through the Payment Portal</a>. We can accept cash/check/Apple Pay/Google Pay/VISA or Mastercard at the ASB/Athletics office.
	<ul>
		<li><em>Any online payments made after 2:00 pm will be updated to Final Forms on the next business day.</em></li>
		<li>ASB Card Fee: $30 for the entire school year, non-refundable</li>
		<li>Athletics Fee: $100 per sport ($125 for football). Some sports will require an additional fee for uniform items that athletes will keep.&nbsp;<em>All fines must be paid before the athletics fee can be paid in the payment portal or in person</em>.</li>
	</ul>
	</li>
