	<header class="entry-header">
		<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.newhartfordyouthhockey.com/2021-2022-rink-procedures/" rel="bookmark">2021-2022 Rink Procedures</a></h2>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		
<p>All,<br>As we start to get back into the rink this week let’s keep in mind some general Covid procedures:<br>The Town of New Hartford and NHYHA recommends all parents and spectators wear masks inside the building. Players and coaches are recommended to as well. Players can remove them during play.<br>We are asking you to be courteous of others and social distance when possible. <br>Please try to limit your time before and after inside the rink. Players and spectators. <br>When possible have the players come partially dressed. <br>Practice and game procedures will be similar to last year. Enter in rear exit through the new 4 doors.
