<li>Perform a chat backup in the original WhatsApp (Settings &gt; Chats &gt; Chat Backup).</li>



<li>While using GB WhatsApp download new version, you will be prompted to &#8220;Restore&#8221;, so click on that option to retrieve the messages.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Step 6: Adjust Settings</h3>



<ul>
<li>Complete setting up your profile by adding a name and a picture.</li>



<li>Look into customization privacy settings, themes, and options like anti-delete or Ghost Mode.</li>
</ul>


<h2 class="kt-adv-heading9_2fe78f-eb wp-block-kadence-advancedheading kt-adv-heading-has-icon" data-kb-block="kb-adv-heading9_2fe78f-eb"><span class="kb-svg-icon-wrap kb-adv-heading-icon kb-svg-icon-fe_fastForward kb-adv-heading-icon-side-left"><svg viewBox="0 0 24 24"  fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg"  aria-hidden="true"><polygon points="13 19 22 12 13 5 13 19"/><polygon points="2 19 11 12 2 5 2 19"/></svg></span><span class="kb-adv-text-inner">How to Back Up and Restore GB WhatsApp</span></h2>


<p>This is a procedure that you can follow to ensure that you keep a record of the download statistics of your GB WhatsApp application whethher you are device-switching, re-installing, or from the use of mods.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Step 1: Back up the chats in the old phone</h3>



<ul>
<li>Access GB WhatsApp APK and click on three dots in the topright corner &gt; Select &#8220;Settings&#8221; and then &#8220;Chats&#8221;.</li>



<li>Click on Chat backup &gt; If you are on Android, choose Google Drive, and iOS users can select iCloud.</li>



<li>Click on Backup to retain the chats alongside the media and settings. Android users can select &#8220;Backup to local storage&#8221; to manually backup.</li>
</ul>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Tip:</h4>



<ul>
<li>Turn on auto backup function (daily/weekly/monthly) from &#8220;Google Drive settings&#8221; or &#8220;iCloud settings&#8221;.</li>



<li>Make sure you have a Wi-Fi connection available to upload your larger files, such as documents, to prevent data charges.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Step 2: Restore Chats on New Device</h3>



<ul>
<li>Download GB WhatsApp APK from the GB WhatsApp Official account.</li>



<li>Launching the app and verifying the number will start the process (the number has to be the same as the backup).</li>



<li>On the prompt, click on &#8220;Restore&#8221; and await the download of the chats from Google Drive/iCloud.</li>
</ul>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Note:</h4>



<p>For those coming from Android with local backups, the GB WhatsApp apps download will pick them up automatically.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Step 3: Solving Issues with Failed Restores</h3>



<ul>
<li>&#8220;Backup not found&#8221;? Check if you are logged into the Google/iCloud account linked to the backup.</li>



<li>Missing media files? For Android, manually transfer the WhatsApp/Media folder from your previous device. For iOS, check your iCloud storage.</li>



<li>Backups might be encrypted? For security purposes, go to WhatsApp Settings &gt; Chats &gt; Chat Backup and toggle &#8220;End-to-end encrypted backup&#8221; to enable end-to-end encrypted backup.</li>
</ul>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading">For Users of GB WhatsApp new version</h4>



<ul>
<li>Backup through GB WhatsApp: Go to Settings &gt; Chats &gt; Chat backup.</li>



<li>Before restoring, transfer the GBWhatsApp/Databases folder to the new device.</li>
</ul>
