The details are as follows:<span id="more-63973"></span></p><div class='code-block code-block-2' style='margin: 8px auto; text-align: center; display: block; clear: both;'>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>NADRA Facilities Online and at One Place through the Pak ID Mobile App</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>National Database &amp; Registration Authority Pakistan facilitates the people of Pakistan with the following:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>No queue, no wait</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All the facilities on your phone</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All NADRA services in one place online Faster and easier than ever</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4>List of Facilities /Services</h4>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>B-Form Family Registration Certificate</li>
<li>ID Wallet: All digital documents saved in the phone</li>
<li>Free viewing of family member details</li>
<li>Amendment and renewal of National Identity Card, NAICOP Pakistan, and Agency Card</li>
<li>Biometric facility for passport application</li>
<li>Biometric authentication for the sale and purchase of Islamabad-registered vehicles and succession certificates</li>
<li>Digital ID</li>
</ol>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>PakID Mobile App Features</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Registration of births, deaths, marriages, and divorces in the Union Councils of all districts of Punjab and selected districts of other provinces.</li>
<li></li>
<li>Soon, registration of districts and union councils across Pakistan will also be available through Pak ID.</li>
<li>Proof of <a href="https://www.glxspace.com/2025/06/06/notification-clarification-life-certificate-no-marriage-certificate-financial-assistance-to-the-family/">Life Certificate</a></li>
<li>NADRA office appointment</li>
<li>Location and address of the NADRA office</li>
<li>Change of application to the executive category</li>
<li>An arms license issued by the federal government and the Punjab province</li>
</ol>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>For help, visit www.nadra.gov.pk or call the helpline +92-52-111786100</p>
