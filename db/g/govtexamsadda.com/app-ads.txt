			<p style="text-align: justify;">Bihar Corona Sahayata App:- Present Lockdown situation to help the workers of Bihar who are living in other states, The Government of Bihar launched a mobile application named as<strong> ‘बिहार कोरोना सहायता app’ or ‘Bihar Corona Sahayata App Download’.</strong> The app is for people of Bihar who have stuck in different states. The main motive of this app is to offer financial help to needy people of Bihar.</p>
<p class="has-text-color has-vivid-red-color" style="text-align: justify;"><span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #ff0000;"><strong>मुख्यमंत्री राहत कोष के माध्यम से बिहार आपदा प्रबंधन विभाग द्वारा बिहार से बिहार के बाहर फंसे लोगो को राज्य सरकार 1000 रुपये की सहायता राशी प्रदान कर रही है, अत: इसको प्राप्त करने के लिए निचे दिए जानकारी को पढ़े और दिए गए लिंक से Application “बिहार कोरोना तत्काल सहायता App “ को डाउनलोड करे।</strong></span></p>
<h2 style="text-align: justify;">Bihar Corona Sahayata App Download Link</h2>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Various workers from Bihar move to other states in search of work. After lockdown, The construction works, factories, etc have been shut down to people who were working in these places as daily wages workers are now jobless and money less. A few days back, We have witnessed scene of workers shifting from metro cities to their native place. <strong>Bihar Corona Sahayata App</strong> will help daily wages workers who were forced to leave the city in absence of work, food, and other essential items. The state government of Bihar finally took initiative to help these people through Bihar <a href="https://www.govtexamsadda.com/%e0%a4%95%e0%a5%8b%e0%a4%b0%e0%a5%8b%e0%a4%a8%e0%a4%be-%e0%a4%b5%e0%a4%be%e0%a4%af%e0%a4%b0%e0%a4%b8-covid-19-%e0%a4%95%e0%a5%8d%e0%a4%af%e0%a4%be-%e0%a4%b9%e0%a5%88-symptom-avoidance/">Corona</a> help App.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">This app was launched by the chief minister of Bihar Nitish Kumar. This step of Bihar government will definitely bring some relief for those who don’t have any work to earn and no money is left with them. Following lockdown is difficult for all but it becomes more though for daily wage workers. This financial aid of 1,000 rupees will help them to survive in this difficult situation.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: justify;">Documents required to submit in Bihar 1000 Help app</h3>
<div class="code-block code-block-2">
<p style="text-align: justify;">Before downloading the app you need to know what are the documents required for the scheme:</p>
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
<li><strong>Aadhaar card</strong>: The beneficiary must have their <strong>Aadhaar card</strong> with them and…</li>
<li><strong>Account details</strong>: The bank account of the beneficiary and it must be in Bihar state.</li>
<li><strong>Photograph</strong>: Photograph of the beneficiary, could be a selfie but the face should be visible clearly.</li>
<li><strong>Mobile number (valid):</strong> Must have a mobile number in which you will receive the OTP (one-time-password)</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify;">यह योजना केवल उन्हीं लोगों के लिए है जो बिहार राज्य से बाहर कोरोना वायरस के चलते फसे हुए है। इसके लिए जरूरी कागजात निम्नलिखित है &#8211;</p>
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
<li>लाभार्थी के आधार कार्ड की प्रति</li>
<li>लाभार्थी के नाम से बैंक खाता जो बिहार राज्य में अवस्थित किसी बैंक के ब्रांच में हो।</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify;">अन्य महत्वपूर्ण बातें</p>
<ul style="text-align: justify;">
<li>लाभार्थी के फोटो (सेल्फी) का मिलान आधार डेटाबेस के फोटो से किया जायेगा अतः आधार का फोटो साफ़ होना चाहिए।</li>
<li>एक आधार संख्या पर एक ही रजिस्ट्रेशन होगा।</li>
<li>मोबाइल नंबर पर प्राप्त OTP मोबाइल APP पर करना होगा।</li>
<li>इससे सम्बंधित सहायता सिर्फ बैंक खाता में ही भेजा जायेगा।</li>
</ul>

<li>Select the language, If it may ask to do so.</li>
<li>Enter mobile number and after that, You will receive an OTP in that number enter same in the application.</li>
<li>Now on next, You need to submit details as it may require and…</li>
<li>Submit correct information otherwise, you will not get any benefits of it.</li>
<li>Upload photograph and it must be visible clearly.</li>
<li>Now submit Online application form.</li>
<li>Once you submit it you may get the registration number.</li>
</ul>
