<h3>Geometry Dash Overview</h3>

<h3>Origins of Geometry Dash</h3>
  Geometry Dash was created by Swedish developer Robert Topala, also known as RobTop. Classic platform games and rhythm titles inspired the idea of combining simple controls with intense music-driven stages.
  <br/>
  The game first launched with a small set of levels and later grew through major updates that added more stages and creative tools.
  <br/>
  Geometry Dash now includes dozens of official stages with different difficulty ratings, from easy introductory levels to extreme demon challenges. Fans also know several related titles such as
  <br/>
  <strong>Geometry Dash Lite</strong>, Geometry Dash Meltdown, Geometry Dash World, and Geometry Dash SubZero, which expand the Geometry Dash world with new songs and layouts.
  <br/>
  As runs progress, the cube can transform into other icons like ships, balls, UFOs, waves, robots, spiders, or swing forms. Each icon changes how movement works and how players react to obstacles.
  <br/>
  Difficulty ranges from simple auto sections to complex demon stages, and the community often discusses routes, patterns, and practice strategies for the hardest levels.
