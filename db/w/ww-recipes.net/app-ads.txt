<p><img data-recalc-dims="1" fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" src="https://i0.wp.com/ww-recipes.net//////wp-content/uploads/2016/03/weight-watchers-caramel-apple-tart-recipe.jpg?resize=614%2C471" alt="weight watchers caramel apple tart recipe" width="614" height="471" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-560" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/ww-recipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/weight-watchers-caramel-apple-tart-recipe.jpg?w=614&amp;ssl=1 614w, https://i0.wp.com/ww-recipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/weight-watchers-caramel-apple-tart-recipe.jpg?resize=300%2C230&amp;ssl=1 300w" sizes="(max-width: 614px) 100vw, 614px" /></p>
<p>This is a nice alternative to the usual <strong><a title="apple" href="https://ww-recipes.net/tag/apple/">apple</a> pie</strong>. The apple and caramel flavors mix together great!</p>
<p><strong><u>Weight Watchers Caramel Apple Tart recipe</u></strong><br />
Makes 8 servings</p>
<!-- Start GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- End GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- Start GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- End GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- Start GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- End GADSWPV-3.17 --><p><strong>Ingredients</strong><br />
2 medium tart green apples (peeled, cored, and thinly sliced)<br />
1 pre-made refrigerated pie crust<br />
1/3 cup chopped pecans<br />
1/3 cup caramel apple dip<br />
2 tablespoons apple jelly<br />
1 tablespoon sugar<br />
1 teaspoon <a title="lemon" href="https://ww-recipes.net/tag/lemon/">lemon</a> zest<br />
1 teaspoon cinnamon<br />
powdered sugar</p>
<p><span id="more-556"></span><br />
<strong>Preparation</strong><br />
1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.<br />
2. Let the pie crust stand to room temperature.<br />
3. Mix together the apple slices sugar, lemon zest and cinnamon; toss to coat.<br />
4. Unfold the pie crust; place on a baking sheet.<br />
5. Spread the caramel apple dip over the crust to within 2 inches of edge.<br />
6. Place the apple mixture over the caramel; sprinkle with pecans.<br />
7. Fold the crust sides about 2 inches up and over the apples, folding where necessary.<br />
8. Bake for about 20 minutes.<br />
9. Melt the apple jelly in a pan over low heat.<br />
10. Remove the tart from the oven and brush the jelly over the entire tart (including the pastry).<br />
11. Sift powdered sugar over the tart before serving.</p>
<p>How many Weight Watchers points in caramel apple tart recipe?<br />
<strong>WW POINTS per serving:</strong> 3<br />
<strong>Nutritional information per serving:</strong> 151 calories, 8.5g fat, 1.6g fiber</p>
