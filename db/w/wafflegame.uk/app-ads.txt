        <h2 id="overview">An Overview of the Waffle Game</h2>

        <h3>How the Gameplay Works</h3>
        <p>
          The core of the Waffle Game is to rearrange scrambled letters on the waffle-shaped grid to form six valid,
          intersecting words (three horizontal and three vertical). The objective is to make the entire board green by
          placing every letter in its correct spot.
        </p>

        <h3>Accessible on Any Platform</h3>
        <p>
          As a browser-based game, Waffle Game is accessible on any device with an internet connection. No download is
          needed—open your browser on your computer, tablet, or phone to play instantly.
        </p>

        <h3>Simple Drag-and-Drop Controls</h3>
        <p>
          Click and drag a letter to swap it with any other letter on the board. Each swap counts as one move, so think
          carefully before you act!
        </p>

        <h3>Understanding the Rules and Colours</h3>
        <p>
          To master the <strong>Waffle Daily Game</strong>, you must understand the colour-coded clues that appear after
          each swap:
        </p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>🟩 Green:</strong> Perfect! This letter is in the right word and the right spot.</li>
          <li><strong>🟨 Yellow:</strong> You're close! This letter is in the word, but it's in the wrong position.</li>
          <li><strong>⬜ Grey:</strong> Not quite. This letter doesn't belong in this word at all.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>
          You have a maximum of 15 moves to solve the puzzle, but every puzzle can be solved in a minimum of 10 moves.
          The fewer moves you use, the more stars you earn!
        </p>

        <h3>Track and Share Your Waffle Score With the Community</h3>
        <p>We keep track of your progress on each run, including rounds played, streaks, total number of stars earned,

        <h2 id="tips">Simple Tips to Master the Waffle Game</h2>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Start with the Green:</strong> Green letters are already in their final positions. Work around
            these fixed points to solve the rest of the puzzle.</li>
          <li><strong>Focus on Intersections:</strong> Letters in the centre of the grid belong to two words. Solving
            these intersection points can quickly unlock multiple clues.</li>
          <li><strong>Plan Your Swaps:</strong> Avoid random swaps. Try to make moves that place a yellow letter into a
            potentially correct spot to solve the puzzle more efficiently.</li>
          <li><strong>Aim for 10 Moves:</strong> Challenge yourself to solve the puzzle in 10 moves or fewer. This
            requires thinking a few steps ahead to earn a perfect 5-star score.</li>
        </ul>
