<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ketogenic_diet">Ketogenic diet</span></h2>
<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/Ketogenic_diet" title="Ketogenic diet">Ketogenic diet</a></b> is a high-fat, moderate-protein, low-carbohydrate diet that has gained popularity for its potential benefits in weight loss, <a href="/wiki/Epilepsy" title="Epilepsy">epilepsy</a> management, and overall health. The primary goal of the ketogenic diet is to put the body into a state of <a href="/wiki/Ketosis" title="Ketosis">ketosis</a>, where it burns fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates. <a href="/wiki/Ketogenic_diet" title="Ketogenic diet">Learn more...</a>
