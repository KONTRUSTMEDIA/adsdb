<p><img fetchpriority="high" decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13480" src="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Touring-Australia-1.jpg" alt="Touring Australia" width="800" height="406" srcset="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Touring-Australia-1.jpg 800w, https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Touring-Australia-1-250x127.jpg 250w, https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Touring-Australia-1-500x254.jpg 500w" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px"/></p>
<p><span style="font-size: revert; color: #000000;">Australians love to travel, self-driving ‘</span><strong style="font-size: revert; color: #000000;"><a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/how-long-drive-around-australia/">The Big Lap’ of the country</a></strong><span style="font-size: revert; color: #000000;"> is high on the bucket list for many.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: revert; color: #000000;">A drive around Australia takes a long time and it’s little wonder that many Aussies spend a year or more on the road, exploring, living in tents, caravans or motorhomes. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: revert; color: #000000;">Towing a boat or packing a kayak can be a part of the adventure.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The drive around Australia covers <strong>14, 935 km / 9,280 miles</strong> via<strong> National Highway 1</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="color: #000000;">Even the little<a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/things-to-do-in-tasmania/"> <strong>island state of  Tasmania</strong> </a>can  take a few months to fully explore and the famous <a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/east-coast-australia/"><strong>East Coast Road Trip</strong></a> from Sydney up to Cairns in Far North Queensland needs about 6 weeks to experience.</span></p>
<p>For many visitors to the country, a self-drive road trip through the outback regions of Australia, such as <a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/self-drive-melbourne-to-uluru-alice-springs/"><strong>Melbourne to Uluru</strong></a> (Ayers Rock) will be a once in a lifetime experience.</p>
<p>We still have many miles of unsealed red-dirt roads in Australia where it is possible to be off the main routes and get to know remote regions of the country.</p>
<p>It is possible to drive the whole way around Australia and keep to sealed roads whilst still seeing the outback as well as the coastal regions, mountains, and cities the country is famous for.</p>

<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"> EXPLORING VICTORIA</span></h1>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12889" src="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Victoria-Australia-Best-attractions-and-places-to-see.jpg" alt="Victoria, Australia - Best attractions and places to see" width="1200" height="500" srcset="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Victoria-Australia-Best-attractions-and-places-to-see.jpg 950w, https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Victoria-Australia-Best-attractions-and-places-to-see-250x104.jpg 250w, https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Victoria-Australia-Best-attractions-and-places-to-see-500x208.jpg 500w" sizes="(max-width: 1200px) 100vw, 1200px"/></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Victoria is a state full of surprises with more than enough natural attractions to keep visitors busy for months.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">From the <a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/melbourne-attractions/"><strong>capital city of Melbourne</strong></a> to the high country, the world-famous <a href="https://explorethegreatoceanroad.com.au/great-ocean-road-map/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Great Ocean Road &amp; 12 Apostles </strong></a>, and some of the best beaches in the country. Australia has it all.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Much of our focus is within Victoria, hiking, <a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/kayaking-melbourne-surrounds/"><strong>kayaking</strong></a>, camping, and creating self-drive itineraries to help visitors plan trips. </span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Did you know it is possible to see <a href="https://hikecamppaddle.com.au/things-to-do-in-melbourne-at-night/"><strong>Little Penguins in Melbourne </strong></a>or, less than an hour’s drive away, you can walk in the beautiful <a href="https://explorethedandenongs.com.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>cool temperate rainforest of the Dandenong Ranges?</strong></a></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">We love road trips and have a number of itineraries that may help others plan their adventures.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">Most of our own travels start in Melbourne, including two a week self-drive tour to see Uluru, the <strong>Flinders Ranges</strong>, camping, and exploring New South Wales and Victoria.</span></p>
