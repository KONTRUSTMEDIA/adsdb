<p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.puzzlesofcolor.com/" ><strong>Puzzles of Color</strong></a> is a vibrant, screen-free solution designed to spark creativity, connection, and a love for art and culture. Puzzles of Color offers thoughtfully curated puzzles featuring artwork by artists of color, with a mission to increase representation in a joyful, inclusive activity. Whether it’s a quiet afternoon at home or an activity the whole family can dive into together, these puzzles provide a colorful, educational escape from screen time.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3866.jpeg" ><img loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-109831 aligncenter" src="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3866-295x300.jpeg" alt="" width="200" height="200" srcset="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3866-150x150.jpeg 150w, https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3866-120x120.jpeg 120w, https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3866-80x80.jpeg 80w" sizes="(max-width: 200px) 100vw, 200px" /></a></p>
<p>Whether you’re selecting a puzzle to enjoy during the holidays or choosing one to give as a gift, Puzzles of Color offers a wonderful selection, including 300-, 500-, and 1,000-piece options.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3869.jpeg" ><img loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-109832 aligncenter" src="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3869-300x241.jpeg" alt="" width="300" height="241" srcset="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3869-300x241.jpeg 300w, https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3869-768x618.jpeg 768w, https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3869.jpeg 915w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>As an avid puzzler myself—I almost always have one in progress on my table—I selected one of the more challenging 1,000-piece designs. I especially enjoyed the intricate detail of Garden Glory by Lauren Younge. I also appreciated that their website allows you to sort puzzles by piece count, difficulty, or theme. You can even create a custom puzzle, which would make an excellent gift for a grandmother.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3870.jpeg" ><img loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-109833 aligncenter" src="https://www.whosaidnothinginlifeisfree.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/IMG_3870-249x300.jpeg" alt="" width="185" height="250" /></a></p>
<p>After my experience, I can confidently say that Puzzles of Color has earned a spot on my list of go-to puzzle brands. Whether you’re shopping for yourself or someone special, you’ll find a puzzle that’s both engaging and beautiful.</p>
