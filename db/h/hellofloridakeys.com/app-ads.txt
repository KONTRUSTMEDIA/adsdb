<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center">About The Blog</h2>



<p>Hello Florida Keys is a blog that covers everything in the Florida Keys. All the way from Key Largo to Key West, we cover the best things to do, restaurants, and everything in between. </p>



<p>Our love of the Florida Keys started when Chelsea interned in Key West for the summer at the age of 19. She was completely captivated and she got Mitchell hooked, too. Now we spend tons of time there as a family with our two babies.</p>



<p>We have been there for every season (I know, most people don&#8217;t consider a place so tropical to have seasons, but it does!), stayed in more accommodations than we can count, and have tried key lime pie from pretty much every restaurant in town. </p>



<p>We know all of the ins and outs of the Florida Keys.  <strong>And we are bringing YOU all of that knowledge.</strong></p>



<p>We have helped dozens of friends and family plan their trip to the Florida Keys. And we&#8217;ve built this blog to help you too!</p>



<p>Whether you’re visiting the Florida Keys for the first time or whether you&#8217;re a regular, there is always something new to discover. We want to help you have the best time in the Florida Keys. </p>
