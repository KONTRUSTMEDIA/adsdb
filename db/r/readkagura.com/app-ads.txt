<p><strong>Kagurabachi</strong>&nbsp;(カグラバチ)&nbsp;is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Takeru Hokazono. It started serialization on September 19, 2023 as part of the second wave of releases under Issue #42, 2023 of Weekly Shonen Jump. This publication follows the previous release of Mama Yuyu, which debuted in the prior issue, Issue #41, and precedes the upcoming release of Two on Ice, set to debut in Issue #43.</p>
