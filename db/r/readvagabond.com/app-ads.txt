Growing up in the late 16th century Sengoku era Japan, <b>Shinmen Takezō</b> is shunned by the local villagers as a devil child due to his wild and violent nature. After the two are separated, Shinmen returns home on a self-appointed mission to notify the Hon&#8217;iden family of Matahachi&#8217;s survival. He instead finds himself a wanted criminal, framed for his friend&#8217;s supposed murder based on his history of violence. Upon being captured, he is strung up on a tree and left to die.
