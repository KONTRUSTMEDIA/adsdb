<p><img decoding="async" alt="Delicious Creamy Lemon Risotto in a Bowl" height="800" data-src="https://biuen.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/img_7794_1.jpg" width="533" title="Perfect Lemon Risotto for Sunday Supper 18" src="data:image/svg+xml;base64,PHN2ZyB3aWR0aD0iMSIgaGVpZ2h0PSIxIiB4bWxucz0iaHR0cDovL3d3dy53My5vcmcvMjAwMC9zdmciPjwvc3ZnPg==" class="lazyload" style="--smush-placeholder-width: 533px; --smush-placeholder-aspect-ratio: 533/800;"></p>

If you’re a local, you already understand the incredible energy and diverse offerings this market provides. It’s a vibrant hub where vendors travel from neighboring states like Pennsylvania and Virginia, alongside our very own Maryland’s Eastern Shore, bringing an astonishing array of fresh produce, artisanal goods, and unique culinary delights.</p>
<p>The moment you pull up under the bridge, your senses are immediately awakened. The rich, inviting aroma of Zeke’s coffee wafts through the air – a scent that promises a delightful start to any Sunday morning. They truly craft some of the best coffee around, a perfect pick-me-up as you begin your market exploration. And for pickle enthusiasts like myself, there’s a legendary vendor who brings barrel upon barrel of every pickle imaginable. Half-sour, full-sour, spicy, sweet, garlic-infused – the list goes on, and I make it a point to visit their stand every single time. It’s an experience that speaks to the heart of local, seasonal eating and community connection.</p>
