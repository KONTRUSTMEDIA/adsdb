<p style="text-align: justify;">Budapest is the capital of Hungary. As the largest city of Hungary, it serves as the country's principal, political, cultural, commercial, industrial and transportation center and is considered an important thermal bath hub in Central Europe. It is the 8th most populous city in the European Union. Straddling a gentle curve int he Danube River, Budapest is flanked by the Buda Hills on its west bank and what is essentially the start of the Great Plain to the east. Due to its geographical features Budapest is considered one of the capitals with the world's most beautiful location.</p>
