A first cross (F1) breed will result in some puppies exhibiting more poodle traits than Cocker Spaniel. This means some coats are more akin to fur than hair.</p><p>13. The hair on Cockerpoos ranges from straight to curled. Look at the <a href="https://cockapoo.me/cockapoo-puppies/">many colours</a>, but the most popular <a href="https://cockapoo.me/black-cockapoo/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">coat colour is black.</a></p><p>14. Cockerpoos may also have a merle coat, (where random portions are diluted to create a mottled appearance). These are common in the USA but not in UK</p><p>15. A Cockerpoo’s fur is <a href="https://cockapoo.me/are-cockapoos-hypoallergenic/">hypoallergenic</a>– meaning they don’t set off an allergic reaction in people.</p><figure id="attachment_2927" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2927" style="width: 225px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://cockapoo.me/cockapoo" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-2927 size-medium cwvlazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAP//////zCH5BAEHAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" data-src="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-f1-225x300.jpg.webp" alt="F1 cockerpoo puppy" width="225" height="300" title="Is A Cockerpoo Right For You? 53 Useful Facts 4" data-srcset="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-f1-225x300.jpg.webp 225w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-f1.jpg.webp 720w" data-sizes="auto, (max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-2927" class="wp-caption-text">F1 Cockapoo Puppy</figcaption></figure><h2>Cockapoo Grooming & Care<strong><a href="https://cockapoo.me/cockapoo-grooming/">(Grooming tips)</a></strong></h2><p>16. Cockerpoos are a low dander, low shedding dog (a trait inherited from the Poodle)</p><p>17. Cockerpoos with dominate Poodle genes will need to be clipped, while Cockapoos with dominate Cocker spaniel genes have hair that will not to be cut</p><p>18. A Cocker-Style coat should be brushed daily to avoid kinking in the hair</p><p>19. A Poodle-Style Coat needs trimming on a regular basis (every 6 – 8 weeks)</p><p>20. Bathing should only be done when necessary to keep the coat’s natural oils and lustre.</p><p>21. Cockerpoo owners should pay particular attention to their large floppy ears that require regular cleaning to prevent infections</p><p>22. If cared for properly Cockerpoos are relatively odourless.</p><figure id="attachment_2928" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2928" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://cockapoo.me/cockapoo" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-2928 size-medium cwvlazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAP//////zCH5BAEHAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" data-src="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-temperament-300x300.jpg.webp" alt="cockerpoo temperament" width="300" height="300" title="Is A Cockerpoo Right For You? 53 Useful Facts 5" data-srcset="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-temperament-300x300.jpg.webp 300w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-temperament-150x150.jpg.webp 150w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-temperament-100x100.jpg.webp 100w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2018/06/cockapoo-temperament.jpg.webp 768w" data-sizes="auto, (max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-2928" class="wp-caption-text">Cockerpoos have a friendly temperament</figcaption></figure><h2>Cockapoo Breeders<strong>[<a href="https://wp.me/P51ojC-zv" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Breeders Pages</a>]</strong></h2><p>23. <a href="https://cockapoo.me/f1-cockapoo/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">An F1 Cockapoo</a> is the first cross with a American or English Cocker Spaniel and a Poodle</p><p>24. An F2 Cockerpoo is the off-spring of two F1 Cockapoos</p><p>25. An F3 Cockerpoo is sired when an F2 is bred to another F2</p><p>26. An F1b or F2b is where a Cockerpoo is bred back to either a Poodle or a Cocker Spaniel giving (depending on which generation the parents were)</p><p>27. <a href="http://www.cockapooclubgb.co.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Cockapoo Club GB</a>, <a href="http://www.americancockapooclub.com/index.asp" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">American Cockapoo Club</a> and Cockapoo Club of America are the official breed clubs and exist to safeguard and improve the breed</p><p>28. A Cockerpoo breeder can determine everything about the size, coat and colour of the dog…but not the sex</p><p>29. There are 27 registered breeders the UK</p><p>30. Cockerpoos are less prone to genetic diseases than pure bred dogs. However, If the sire (male) and dam (female) are not screened before mating, they can pass a genetic disorder to the off-spring</p><p>31. The most common genetic disorder for the Cockerpoo is PRA (Progressive Retinal Atrophy) which causes blindness</p><p>32. Others common genetic disorders are: Von Willebrand disease (vWD), a bleeding disorder; retinal dysplasia, eyes; phosphofructokinase (PFK) which affects the muscles; familial nephropathy (FN), renal disease, hip dysplasia, hip joints; primary and secondary glaucoma, (primary glaucoma is a inherited eye disorder).</p><figure id="attachment_2064" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2064" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://cockapoo.me/cockapoo" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="wp-image-2064 size-medium cwvlazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAP//////zCH5BAEHAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" data-src="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2016/04/2-cute-cockapoos-300x198.jpg.webp" alt="Cockerpoo puppies for sale" width="300" height="198" title="Is A Cockerpoo Right For You? 53 Useful Facts 6" data-srcset="https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2016/04/2-cute-cockapoos-300x198.jpg.webp 300w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2016/04/2-cute-cockapoos-768x506.jpg.webp 768w, https://cockapoo.me/wp-content/uploads/cwv-webp-images/2016/04/2-cute-cockapoos.jpg.webp 960w" data-sizes="auto, (max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px"></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-2064" class="wp-caption-text">Healthy Cockapoo Puppies</figcaption></figure><h2>Cockerpoo Exercise</h2><p>33.
