<p>Hi Friends,</p>



<p>Today I am sharing with you a mouthwatering Pickle dish recipe made with   Katbel (Wood apple). It is a famous street food of kolkata generally found in winter season. It can be easily made at home. You need only few ingredients for making this beautiful pickle dish.</p>



<figure class="wp-block-image"><img decoding="async" width="1024" height="711" src="http://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015-1024x711.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3088" srcset="https://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015-1024x711.jpg 1024w, https://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015-600x417.jpg 600w, https://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015-300x208.jpg 300w, https://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015-768x533.jpg 768w, https://www.cookingenuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/IMG-Nov292019at085015.jpg 1440w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" /></figure>



<p>Preparation time &#8211;  10 minutes.  Serve &#8211; 4</p>



<!-- Start GADSWPV-3.17 --><div id="ga_14556849"> </div><div style="text-align:right; width:300px; padding:5px 0;">
   <img src="http://bcdn.grmtas.com/images/gourmetads-logo.jpg" alt="logo" style="float:right; border:none;" />

   </div>
</div><!-- End GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- Start GADSWPV-3.17 --><!-- End GADSWPV-3.17 --><p>Ingredients &#8211;</p>



<p>1.Wood apple (ripe)-  1</p>



<p>2.Salt to taste.</p>



<p>3.Black salt &#8212;&#8211;1/2 tsp.</p>



<p>4.Sugar &#8211;  5/6 tbsp.</p>



<p>5.Green chilly &#8211;  2 (finely chopped)</p>



<p>6.Grated ginger &#8211; 1 tsp.</p>



<p>7.Coriander leaves (finely chopped)  &#8211; 4 tbsp.</p>



<p>8.Mustard sauce (kashundi)  &#8211;  2 tbsp.</p>



<p>9.Mustard oil &#8211; 2 tbsp.</p>



<p>Instructions &#8211;</p>



<p>Wash and crack the wood apple and scoop out the brown flesh(pulp) within. In a large glass bowl put the flesh and mash it into a smooth paste. To this add salt, black salt, sugar, green chilly, coriander leaves, mustard sauce and oil. Mix all the ingredients with a ladle/spoon(vigorously). Now the Wood apple pickle is ready to serve. For better taste you can keep the pickle in direct sunlight for 4 to 5 hours.</p>



<p>Hope you will like the recipe. Enjoy.</p>
