<p>People just pile up old bricks and use them for repairs. But do you know you can get up to $200 – 300 for an old brick? That&#8217;s true! In fact, I have also seen some vintage bricks sell for $800.</p>
<h3><span class="ez-toc-section" id="How_Much_Does_1_Brick_Cost"></span>How Much Does 1 Brick Cost?<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h3>
<p>Building (structural) brick that is used in construction and has holes to save material and reduce weight, costs $0.55-0.75 cents each. Thin brick, also known as brick veneer is the most expensive option. Prices average $7-8.5 per square foot.</p>

<p><b>Regular bricks will crack at high temperatures and can cause a real accident if used in fire pits</b>. Instead, you are going to use firebricks, also called refractory bricks. This type of brick is much denser than regular bricks and goes through a kiln process to be able to withstand high temperatures.</p>
