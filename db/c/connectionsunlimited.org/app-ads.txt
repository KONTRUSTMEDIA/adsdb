The groups might be related to certain themes such as fish, fire-related terms, etc., and they can be as straightforward or tricky.</p><h3 id="help-section" class="for-content">How to Play Connections?</h3><img class="ill" src="https://www.connectionsunlimited.org/how-to-play-connections.png" alt="how to play connections game"/><ul><li><strong>Step 1:</strong> The game presents various items, from which you have to find groups of four that share something in common.</li><li><strong>Step 2:</strong> Once you identify a group, select the four items and tap 'Submit' to check if your guess is correct.</li><li><strong>Step 3:</strong> The goal is to find all groups without making 4 mistakes!</li></ul><p><strong>Additional Help:</strong> Keep in mind that categories will always be more specific than "5-letter words", "names", or "verbs." <strong>Categories might be tricky!</strong>. Each identified group is assigned a color, which is revealed as you solve.
