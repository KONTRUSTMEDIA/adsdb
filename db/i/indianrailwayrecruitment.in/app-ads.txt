<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #ff0000;">Railway Recruitment 2026 Group Wise Posts Detail</span></h2>
<p>Indian Railway Board, usually announces Group Wise recruitment notification. All posts under the railway department are divided into Gazetted and Non-Gazetted categories. Gazetted include (Group &#8216;A&#8217; and &#8216;B&#8217;) Posts and Non-Gazetted Include (Group &#8216;C&#8217; and &#8216;D&#8217;) Posts. Below we mentioned Group-wise Posts and Education Qualification details.</p>
<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Group A</td>
<td>The posts under the Group A category are usually carried out by UPSC by conducting Civil Service Exam, Engineering Service Exam, and Combined Medical Service Examination.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Group B</td>
<td>The Group B posts link Section Officers Grade &#8211; upgraded posts from Group &#8216;C&#8217; railway employees on a deputation basis.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Group C</td>
<td>The posts under the Group C category usually Include Station Master, Ticket Collector, Clerk, Commercial Apprentice, Safety staff, Traffic Apprentice, Engineering posts (Electrical, Signal and telecommunication, Civil, Mechanical), etc.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Group D</td>
<td>The posts under the Group D category include <a href="https://indianrailwayrecruitment.in/railway-track-maintainer/">Track-man</a>, Helper, Assistant Points Man, Safaiwala / Safaiwali, Gunman, Peon, and various Posts in various cells and boards of railway department.</td>
</tr>
