	<h3>Population Served</h3>
<p>Residents of the Village of Indian Hill and Indian Hill School District, as well as students who attend Cincinnati Country Day.</p>
<h3>Purpose</h3>
<p>The purpose of the Indian Hill Recreation Commission is to provide recreational opportunities and athletic programs for residents of the Village of Indian Hill and the students of the Indian Hill School District and Cincinnati Country Day School.</p>
<h3>Goals</h3>
<p>The goals of all IHRC Recreational and Athletic programs are as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>To encourage community spirit and friendship,</li>
<li>To allow all participants an opportunity to participate,</li>
<li>To instruct participants in the development of their capabilities,</li>
<li>To build good character and encourage teamwork,</li>
<li>To develop and practice good sportsmanship,</li>
<li>To implement the principles of fair play,</li>
<li>To improve physical fitness,</li>
<li>And most importantly, to have fun!</li>
</ul>
